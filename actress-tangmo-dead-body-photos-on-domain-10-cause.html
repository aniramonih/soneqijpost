The untimely death of the Actress Tangmo, who was just 37 years old when she passed away on February 26, 2022. Her sudden demise came as a shock to her fans and the entire entertainment industry.

On February 24, 2022, Tangmo was enjoying a boat trip with her manager and four others when she fell from a speedboat into the Chao Phraya river in Bangkok, Thailand.

After the Actress went missing, her boyfriend and brother joined the rescue team in their desperate search for her throughout the night.

Finally, on February 26, 2022, her brother found her lifeless body about 300 meters from Pibulsongkram pier in Nonthaburi province after a 38-hour-long search.

Later, on April 26, 2022, the Royal Thai Police ruled out foul play in her death but charged six people with “recklessness” and involvement in her demise.

The charges clear light on the irresponsible and dangerous behavior of some individuals on the boat, leading to Tangmo’s tragic accident.

Tangmo Cause Of Death – Case details

According to Bangkok Post, the statement made by the Head of the National Police, Suwat Jangyodsuk, the untimely passing of Thai Actress Nida appears to have resulted from an accident, according to the evidence. Nida’s body was found in a river two days after falling from a speedboat.

The Police have questioned at least 29 witnesses. They are examining witness statements and other evidence, such as the speedboat’s GPS data and phone records, as part of the investigation.

Two suspects, Paiboon “Robert” Treekanchanana and Tanuphat “Por” Lertthaweewit, were charged and pleaded guilty to several offenses, including driving a boat without a legal certificate, having an expired license, being a boat skipper without a legal certificate, and littering in the river.

However, the remaining four suspects, Wisapat “Sand” Manomairat, Nitat “Job” Kiratisut Sathorn, Itsarin “Gatick” Jutha Suksawat, and Phim “Em” Thamtheerasri, denied all charges filed against them.

Panida, Nida’s mother, was surprised by the confessions of Por and Robert, given their previous denial of all charges during the year-long case.

Tangmo’s manager, Sand, is seeking a 40.8 million baht lawsuit against Tangmo’s mother in response to her initial filing of murder charges against him, which were later dropped.
