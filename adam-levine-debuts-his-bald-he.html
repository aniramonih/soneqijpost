Adam Levine Debuts His Bald Head on The Voice: See Twitter's Funniest Reactions

Maroon 5 frontman and The Voice coach Adam Levine has rocked several extreme hairdos in the past: He's dyed his man-locks platinum blond and shorn them all off into a tight buzz cut. He's even had his wife, model Behati Prinsloo, cut his hair. But none of Levine's previous hair-dares prepped the Twitter-verse for the television debut of his completely hairless, very shiny, bald head.

Levine was quick to make the distinction, though, that he is not bald—he has a shaved head. "Excuse me. Lex Luthor was bald," he wrote on Twitter last night after his new 'do premiered on The Voice. "I have 'shaved' my head. Meaning, shaved by choice. Big difference. OK?!! OK?!!" But whether you call it bald, shaved, or hairless, Levine's fans had plenty to say about his bare head. Some of the reactions were less than positive (some even downright mean), but most were some combination of shock, humor, and approval.
