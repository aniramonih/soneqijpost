Who is Elite Actor Adam Nourou?

19 years old Adam Nourou (born on December 12, 2002) is a French actor whose fame rose to new heights of stardom after his fabulous performance in the role of Bilal in the Netflix series “Elite Season 5.”

He described his experience working with the cast of “Elite” as memorable. During the show, he made many new friends like André Lamoglia, Valentina Zenere, Carla Díaz, Claudia Salas, Omar Ayuso, Itzan Escamilla and Georgina Amorós.

In 2021 Nourou received the award for the Best New Actor at Goya Awards Ceremony for his performance in “Adu,” a Salvador Calvo movie came in 2020.

Childhood, Parents, and Brothers

Adam Nourou (age 19) took birth in a family of Comoros origin in Étampes, France, on December 12, 2002. Scrolling his Facebook account, we found a photograph where we can see his father, mother, and siblings; both are Comorian, yet their names are unavailable.

In an interview with Vanity Teen, Adam said that he was only 10 when a casting saw him and offered him a role in “Turf.” He was studying at Collège de Guinette at that time. As of 2022, he achieved so much that he is the only black guy to win a Goya Award.

Acting Career

After appearing in “Turf,” many new doors opened for him. 2 years after the first acting project, he received a role in “I’m All Yours” as Un copain de Donnadieu à seven ans. He established contacts with many talent management agencies like Paris-based Agencies Artistiques.

In 2020, he got to play Massar in “Adu,” an award-winning production of Álvaro Augustin. With this film, his popularity grew exponentially, and many casting agents started keeping an eye on his work. In 2022 he worked as a presenter for “36 Premios Goya.”

Undoubtedly, his work is commendable, and the future is very bright for this actor, both in the television and movie industry.

Personal Life

Nourou has done many acting jobs, and a few times, his name got attached to his co-stars. He never responded to any rumors, and as far as we came to know, he is not linked to any particular girl.

Indeed, he enjoys a busy social life like attending parties with co-actors and appearing at ceremonies, but he never came to any event with a female partner. So, it is safe to assume that he is single and has no girlfriend.
