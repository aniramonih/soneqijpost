Age, Biography and Wiki

Al Haymon is an American boxing promoter and manager. He is the founder and CEO of Premier Boxing Champions, a boxing promotion company. He is also the manager of several prominent boxers, including Floyd Mayweather Jr., Adrien Broner, and Andre Berto. Haymon was born in Cleveland, Ohio, and grew up in the city's Glenville neighborhood. He attended Glenville High School and later graduated from Harvard University with a degree in economics. Haymon began his career in the music industry, working as a talent manager for artists such as Mary J. Blige, LL Cool J, and Heavy D. He then moved into boxing, becoming a manager and promoter. He has since become one of the most powerful figures in the sport, with his fighters winning numerous world titles. Haymon is estimated to be worth around $200 million. He is married and has two children.
