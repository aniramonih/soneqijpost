Amanda Williams Shot To Death in Warrensville Heights, Ohio,

Police are investigating the murder of a 46-year-old Warrensville Heights woman.

According to a release from the city, on Monday around 11:41 p.m. they responded a domestic disturbance to the 19700 block of Sunset Drive.

When police arrived, they found a woman with multiple gunshot wounds.

The woman was taken to Cleveland Clinic-South Pointe Hospital, where she died from her injuries.

The Cuyahoga County Medical Examiner has identified the victim as Amanda Williams.

According to the city, police have a man in custody at the Warrensville Heights jail in connection to the shooting.

The man was identified by the city as Tirrell Edwards.

19 News has reached out to Warrensville Heights police for additional information.
