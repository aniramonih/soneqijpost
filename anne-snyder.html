Anne Snyder Career

Anne is the editor-in-chief of Comment magazine. She oversees the Comment magazine project, Breaking Ground. Anne directed  The Philanthropy Roundtable‘s Character Initiative from 2016 to 2019.  The Philanthropy Roundtable is a program seeking to help foundations and business leaders strengthen “the middle ring” of morally formative institutions.

In 2019, she published The Fabric of Character: A Wise Giver’s Guide to Renewing our Social and Moral Landscape. Anne is also a 2020 Emerson Fellow, a Senior Fellow of The Trinity Forum, and a Fellow at the Center for Opportunity Urbanism, a Houston-based think tank that explores how cities can drive opportunity for the bulk of their citizens.

Anne has also worked for  Laity Lodge and the H.E. Butt Family Foundation in Texas from 2014 and 2017. Prior to that, she was previously working for the Ethics and Public Policy Center, World Affairs Journal, and The New York Times.
