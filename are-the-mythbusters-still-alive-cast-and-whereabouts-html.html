Are the MythBusters Still Alive? Cast and Whereabouts

Spread the love             

It seems like you’re looking for information about the current status of the “MythBusters” cast members and their activities post-show. As of my last update in April 2023:

Jamie Hyneman –

Jamie Hyneman, one of the original co-hosts of “MythBusters,” is still alive. After the show ended, he continued to engage in various projects, including working on new inventions and speaking engagements. He is known for his expertise in special effects and engineering.

 

Adam Savage –

Adam Savage is also still alive and has been very active in the science and maker communities. He hosts “Savage Builds,” a show on the Science Channel, and has a popular YouTube channel, “Tested,” where he engages in various building and science-related projects. Additionally, he co-hosts the podcast “Still Untitled: The Adam Savage Project.”

Grant Imahara –

Sadly, Grant Imahara passed away in July 2020 due to a brain aneurysm. He was known for his expertise in electronics and robotics. Before his untimely death, Grant had been a part of the “MythBusters” team and also hosted the Netflix show “White Rabbit Project.”

 

Kari Byron –

Kari Byron, who was part of the “MythBusters” build team, is alive and has continued her career in television. She has hosted and appeared in several science-related shows, including “Head Rush,” “The White Rabbit Project,” and “Crash Test World.”

Tory Belleci –

Tory Belleci, another member of the “MythBusters” build team, is also alive. After “MythBusters,” he worked with Kari Byron and Grant Imahara on “The White Rabbit Project.” Tory has been involved in various television projects and continues to work in the field of science entertainment.

Scottie Chapman and Jessi Combs –

Scottie Chapman left “MythBusters” early in the series and pursued a career in metal fabrication and as a dental hygienist. Jessi Combs, who briefly joined the build team, tragically passed away in August 2019 in a jet-car crash while attempting to break a land speed record.

Overall, the original hosts of “MythBusters,” Jamie Hyneman and Adam Savage, continue to be active in science and engineering-related fields, while sadly, two of the later team members, Grant Imahara and Jessi Combs, have passed away.
