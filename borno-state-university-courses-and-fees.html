Borno State University Courses and Fees

Borno State University is a renowned educational institution in Nigeria located in the northeastern region of the country. It offers a wide range of courses that cater to the educational needs of thousands of students every year. From humanities to sciences, the university aims to equip students with the necessary knowledge and skills needed to succeed in their chosen fields.

Borno State University offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines. Some popular undergraduate courses include Accounting, Business Administration, Computer Science, English Language, Mass Communication, Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics, and Economics, among others. The university also offers postgraduate programs in several fields such as Agriculture, Education, Environmental Science, Political Science, and Public Administration, to name a few. This article will explore the courses offered at Borno State University, the fees payable, and the cutoff mark for admission at the institution.

Borno State University Courses

The institution was established in 2016 with 4 faculties and over 20 departments. With time, other faculties and departments will be added. The courses offered in this university are designed to provide students with the necessary knowledge and skills they are supposed to learn in their chosen fields of study.

Borno State University Fees

The average fee you’ll pay to study at Borno State University can vary depending on the program, level of study, and residency status of the student. It is important to note that exact figures may differ and it is always advisable to check the official website or contact the university directly for accurate and up-to-date information. However, here is a general overview of the fees one should expect to pay at Borno State University:

  • Tuition Fees: This is the primary fee that students are required to pay for their academic programs. The tuition fees can vary based on the program and level of study (undergraduate or postgraduate). For example, undergraduate programs may have lower fees compared to postgraduate programs.

The tuition fees for Nigerian students are typically lower than those for international students. Also, at Borno State University, tuition fees vary depending on the courses one is studying. Their tuition fee is between N30,000 to N54,000 for Nigerian students and N134,000 for international students.

  • Accommodation Fees: The cost of accommodation in university hostels is an additional expense. The fees for hostels are relatively affordable, with prices at ₦30,000 per session.
  • Acceptance and Registration Fees: These are one-time fees paid during the admission process. The acceptance fee reserves the student’s place at the university, while registration fees cover administrative costs. At Borno State University, the acceptance fee is N10,000.
  • Miscellaneous Fees: Students should budget for other costs, including textbooks, stationery, transportation, and personal expenses in addition to the fees mentioned above. Depending on the person’s tastes and lifestyle, these prices may change. There may be additional fees for services such as library access, lab usage, sports facilities, health services, student union fees, and identity cards.

It is important to note that fees are subject to change, and students should always verify the specific fees for their chosen program and academic year directly with the university. Additionally, there might be scholarship opportunities or financial aid programs available to students, so it is advisable to explore such options as well.

Borno State University Cutoff Mark

The cutoff mark for Borno State University (also known as BOSU) may vary each year depending on various factors such as the number of applicants and the capacity of the university, and the performance of applicants at JAMB UTME exams. The cutoff mark for Borno State University is 140.

There are also particular cutoff marks for each course. It is best to contact the university directly or visit their official website for the most accurate and up-to-date information regarding the cutoff mark for admission. However, the general JAMB cut off mark for the institution can still get one admission into the university.
