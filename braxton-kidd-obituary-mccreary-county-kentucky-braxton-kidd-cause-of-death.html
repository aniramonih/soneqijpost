Braxton Kidd Obituary, Death – On September 1, 2011, Michael Todd Kidd, a 42-year-old Whitley City, Kentucky native, passed away at the Roane Medical Center in Harriman, Tennessee. Kentucky was Michael Todd Kidd’s home state at first. The funeral services were conducted by the Reverend Braxton King on Sunday, September 4, 2011 at 7:00 p.m. at the McCreary County Funeral Home. The deceased was remembered at the services. That was followed immediately by the Masonic services.

His parents were Ronald (Ronnie) and Helen (King) Kidd, and he was born on March 11, 1969, in Somerset, Kentucky. They refer to him as their son. King was his mother’s maiden name, which he adopted. Apart from his employment with the Job Corps as the Program Manager for Urban Forestry, he was also a member of the Greenwood Masonic Lodge #903 in Greenwood. Patricia King Bryant and Tammy Kidd, two of his sisters, died before him. He predeceased both of them.

His wife, Traci (Phillips) Kidd, of Whitley City, Kentucky; sons, Lance C. Kidd, a member of the US Air Force in North Carolina; Gavin W. Kidd and Nicholas J. In addition, both their sister Teresa and her spouse Johnny reside in the state of Georgia.

Greenwood Lodge #903, situated in Greenwood, is in charge of the Masonic Service. McCreary County Funeral Home will now be in charge of putting the essential plans into place.
