Early Life

Chris Martin was born and grew up in Whitestone in the Devon section of England to Anthony Martin and Alison Martin. Born on March 2nd in 1977, Chris is the oldest of five siblings. His father served as an accountant and his mother, a music teacher.

His affection in music was seen at a very early age during his preparatory education. That’s why he was shifted to Sherborne School in Dorset to pursue his music classes. There, he met Phil Harvey, the current Coldplay manager.

Chris completed his higher education from University College London (UCL), where he met his Coldplay band-mates Johnny Buckland and Will Champion.

Personal Life

Chris married the US actress Gwyneth Paltrow on December 5, 2003. They met in a band show in 2002. The couple was blessed with two children.

After spending ten years together, both decided to break their relationship in March 2014. They finally divorced in 2016.

Age, Height, and Weight

Career

Chris kicked off his professional music career during his graduation. At UCL, he met Johnny Buckland and decided to start a band. Chris was the lead singer, and Johnny was the guitarist. Soon, they add Guy Berryman as the bass player and Will Champion as the drummer in their band. Together they created the rock band Pectoralz (Coldplay) in 1996.

Their debut album Parachutes came in 2000. The album got success all over the world, and the song Yellow from Parachutes hit the charts at Number 4.

His first solo work came in 2004 in Embrace’s Gravity. In 2006, he collaborated with rapper Jay-Z in the album Kingdom Come. Over the course, he has collaborated with many bands like The Chainsmokers (2016) and DJ Avicii (2015).

In a personal interview, Chris revealed that the idea of his band Coldplay was influenced by Scottish rock band Travis and English rock band Radiohead.

Chris has also worked in numerous films in his career. In the film, Shaun of the Dead, Chris, and his band-mate Johnny had made a cameo appearance as the supporters of ZombAid.

In 2006, Chris made a theme song supporting British conservative party leader David Cameron. The song was entitled “Talk to David.”

Awards & Achievement

Chris is one of the most high profile musicians in the UK. His band Coldplay is the World’s Best-Selling Music Band, having sold over 90 million records worldwide. Chris got his first Grammy Award nomination in 2000 for his song Yellow from the album Parachutes. He later won the award in the best rock song category. Chris has also won two more Grammy Awards for A Rush of Blood to the Head and Viva la Vida. He was also honored with the Brit Award.
In 2017, Debrett named him as one of the most influential people in the UK.
