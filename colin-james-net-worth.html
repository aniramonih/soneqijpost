What is Colin James' Net Worth?

Colin James is a Canadian singer, songwriter, guitarist, and record producer who has a net worth of $8 million. Colin James was born in Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada in August 1964. His styles include rock, blues, R&B, and swing. James released his self-titled debut studio album in 1988. He has also released the albums Sudden Stop, Colin James and the Little Big Band, Bad Habits, National Steel, Colin James and the Little Big Band II, Fuse, Traveler, Limelight, Colin James & The Little Big Band 3, Colin James & The Little Big Band: Christmas, Rooftops and Satellites, Fifteen, Hearts of Fire, and Blue Highways.

His single "Just Came Back" reached #5 in Canada. James has collaborated with artists including Bonnie Raitt, The Chieftains and more. He has won six Juno Awards and been nominated for nine more. James also plays saxophone, piano, and harmonica.
