Early Life

Drew was born in Los Angeles, California. Her father’s name was John Drew Barrymore Jr., who was an actor, and her mother was Ildiko Jaid. She completed her studies studying at Fountain Day School in West Hollywood and Country School.

Age, Height, and Weight

Career

Barrymore made her acting debut at the age of 4 in the movie “The altered States” (1980). She did one of her best roles of Gertie at the age of 7 in “E.T.: The Extraterrestrial” (1982). She came to limelight with that, and her acting skills were praised in such a little age.

But she got spoilt due to her mother, who started taking her to nightclubs, and as a result, Drew was addicted to drugs at the age of 13. She wrote about her this experience in her book Little Girl Lost.

Success came to Drew in the year 1995 when she started her own production company Flower Films. In the same year, she did a brilliant performance in “Boys on the Side.” In the very next year in 1996, she played a memorable role in the blockbuster movie “Scream.” In 1998, she did a couple of good romantic films named “The Wedding Singer” and “Ever After.”

The 2000 movie “Charlie’s Angels” was a turning point in her career as it was a big blockbuster, and her role was appreciated there. She was the producer of the movie, too, and her production company tasted the desired success also. The second part of the film “Charlie’s Angels: Full Throttle” was a blockbuster also. Some of her other famous movies are 50 First Dates, Lucky You, Beverly Hills Chihuahua, Distance, Blended, Miss You Already. She is not just a movie star but a famous model too.

Awards & Achievements
