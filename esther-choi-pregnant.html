Is Esther Choi Pregnant In 2023? Meet Her Husband and Family

Esther Choi is a renowned chef and restaurant owner who has gained popularity as a guest star in Uncle Roger’s videos. She is also known as Auntie Esther, a nickname given by Uncle Roger himself. Many fans are curious about her personal life, especially her pregnancy status and her family background. Here is what we know about Esther Choi.

Is Esther Choi Pregnant in 2023?

No, Esther Choi is not pregnant. Following her participation in a recent First We Feast video titled “How Hot Ones Legend Smokin’ Ed Currie Grows the World’s Hottest Peppers | Heat Eaters,” rumors started to circulate.

Thanks to Esther’s choice of baggy clothing, several keen-eyed observers believed they spotted a possible baby bump. But before we draw any hasty judgments, it’s important to remember that Esther Choi hasn’t yet made an official statement about her pregnancy.

She doesn’t appear to have a baby bump in any of her most recent photos, which strengthens the case against the pregnancy rumors. Rather than being a calculated attempt to hide anything, it appears that the loose clothing was more a question of comfort or style. Thus, in spite of all the talk and rumors, Auntie Esther is not now pregnant.

How Did Esther Choi Start Her Career?

Renowned chef Esther Choi, who finds inspiration in her Korean background, is the chef/owner of mkbar and mkbar Brooklyn in addition to being Ms. Yoo’s partner. Chef Esther believes that cuisine is the finest way to portray a country’s culture.

Thus, she is inspired by her Korean grandmother’s traditional culinary techniques. Since Chef Esther is passionate about teaching New Yorkers the flavors of Korean culture, her food blends traditional and modern influences with seasonal, fresh ingredients.

Esther began her formal education when she was fourteen years old. She worked at Ilili, La Esquina, and the Food Network after completing her professional culinary training at the Institute of Culinary Education (ICE) in New York City.

With her culinary prowess and mouthwatering Korean cuisine, Eater’s “Kitchen Gadgets” video series drew in over 50 million viewers, with Esther starring as the host.
