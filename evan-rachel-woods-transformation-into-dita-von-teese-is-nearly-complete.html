Evan Rachel Woods Dita Von Teese transformation nearly complete


A few years ago, actress Evan Rachel Wood was a fresh-faced blond ingenue who got attention for her mature acting ability and beautiful singing voice, landing her roles in films such as “13” and “Across the Universe.” Today, she’s more famous for being the 19-year-old girlfriend of rock freakazoid Marilyn Manson. Since hooking up with Manson, we’ve noticed some uncanny changes in Evan’s appearance. It started with her change in makeup, and now she’s dyed her blond hair dark and started dressing like a 1950’s pin-up girl – not unlike a certain ex-wife of Manson. At her recent appearance on the red carpet to promote her new film “The Wrestler,” Wood was a dead ringer for Dita Von Teese. Though she looked lovely, it was a little creepy.

Evan Rachel Wood is glorious in a wonderfully deconstructed two-toned Lanvin dress at the photocall for her new movie, The Wrestler, at the Piazzale del Casino during the 2008 Venice Film Festival on Friday in Venice, Italy.

The 20-year-old actress plays estranged daughter Stephanie to Randy “Ram” Robinson (Mickey Rourke), a retired professional wrestler.

The Wrestler is directed by Darren Aronofsky (Rachel Weisz’s husband) and doesn’t have a release date yet.
