Fred Lewis stacks up a hefty net worth on Gold Rush after 14-year army career

Fred’s Journey from the Army to Gold Mining Fred Lewis, a familiar face on the hit TV show Gold Rush, has made quite a name for himself. But did you know that before striking gold, he spent 14 years serving in the army? Let’s delve into the life of this former soldier turned gold miner.

A Soldier’s Struggles After a long and dedicated service in the armed forces, Fred found it challenging to adapt to civilian life. The transition wasn’t easy for him, but little did he know that his life was about to take an unexpected turn.

From American Ninja Warrior to Gold Rush In 2017, Fred Lewis decided to test his mettle by participating in American Ninja Warrior, featuring in seasons 9 and 10. However, his journey into reality TV didn’t stop there. Fred received a call that would change his life forever – an invitation to join the Gold Rush team led by Parker Schnabel.

 Fred Lewis on Gold Rush

Since joining the Gold Rush cast in 2019, Fred has faced numerous challenges in the unpredictable world of gold mining. From financial worries to soaring fuel prices and equipment shortages, he’s seen it all. Fred has also collaborated with fellow miners like Tony Beets and Brandon Clayton, working hard to strike gold.

A Family Affair Fred’s wife, Khara, and their son have also made appearances on the Discovery show, showcasing the family’s journey in the gold mining industry. You can follow Fred on Instagram at @officialfredlewis and Khara at @kharalewis.

Fred Lewis’ Net Worth

Despite the hurdles he’s encountered on Gold Rush, Fred Lewis has managed to amass an impressive net worth. While some of his fellow cast members, like Parker Schnabel and Tony Beets, started their mining careers earlier in life and boast higher net worths, Fred’s estimated net worth in 2022 stands at a commendable $1.6 million, according to Techie Gamers and Stars Offline.

Fred Lewis’s journey from a 14-year army career to the world of gold mining is a testament to his resilience and determination.
