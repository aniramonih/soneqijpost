Gibi ASMR Biography And Net Worth

Gibi ASMR born Gibi Klein, is an American ASMR performer, YouTube personality, Twitch streamer, and cosplayer.

Gibi Klein was born on December 19, 1994, in Chicago, in the United States of America.

Gibi graduated from Northwestern University’s School of Communication with a Bachelor of Science in Film and has a background in theater and film.

Gibi was a high school sophomore with anxiety and insomnia when YouTube’s recommendation algorithm introduced her to the ASMR genre.

After watching and listening to ASMR for years, Gibi created her YouTube channel in June 2016, before her senior year of college.

She began cosplaying and attending anime conventions; she incorporated these interests into role-playing ASMR videos, which feature both existing and original characters.

Currently, her channel, Gibi ASMR, has gathered a whopping 3.16 million subscribers and over a billion views.
