Grammy-Winning Producer Nitti Gritti Teams with Pop Singer Dylan Matthew on Nostalgic Single &quot;Drive

Nitti Gritti, a Grammy-winner, and 12x Platinum producer made his debut on Monstercat alongside returning Gold-certified singer-songwriter Dylan Matthew, with their infectious new single, “Drive You Crazy.”

As the press release notes, the nostalgic song is a callback to falling in love for the first time and feeling the insatiable high of an endless summer. Accompanied by a rush of endorphins in the music video, the pair blend the best of their talents with matured dance sensibilities, catchy vocals, and pop-punk guitar chords, cementing the track as an instant hit.

Nitti Gritti shares, “This is a really feel-good summer record that provides some nostalgia of your first summer romance.”

Dylan Matthew adds, “We wanted to encapsulate what the song is about in the music video – Malibu, the beach, summer love, nostalgia, and California. We’re excited to put this one out, hope you guys like it!”
