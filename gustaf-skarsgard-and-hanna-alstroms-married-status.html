Gustaf Skarsgard and Hanna Alstrom's Married Status

Gustaf Skarsgard is the greatest Swedish actor all the time. He is famous for his role as Floki the boat builder in the TV series Vikings on History Channel. Also, he was the cast member of the HBO series Westworld, playing the role of Karl Strand in the second season.

More than television series, his films credits are Evil (2003), The Way Back (2010) and Kon-Tiki (2012). Being a public figure, the actor has already stolen the hearts of many viewers who want to know his personal life. But sadly he is a divorced man. Keep reading to find out about his love life.  

Six Years of Marital Life With Actress, Hanna Alström

Gustaf, the child of Stellan Skarsgard, and My Skarsgård was in a romantic long term conjual relationship with Hanna Alström. She is a Swedish actress and is now 40 years old.

The lovebirds first met on the set of horror three-part TV Mini-Series, 'Skuggornas hus' back in 1996. They played side roles where Skarsgard played the of Julius "JB" Blumendorph and Hanna as Betty. Later they even worked on a short film.

Suddenly, their friendship turned into a relationship and got married in the year 1996 after being friends for three years. The knot was quite private. However, the duo separated after six years of nuptials in 2006. Together, the former spouses did not share any kids.
