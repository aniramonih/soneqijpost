Gwyneth Paltrow Is &quot;Deeply Loved&quot; by Friends Post-Split, BFF Says: Pic

After her split from husband Chris Martin, Gwyneth Paltrow is getting by with a little help from her friends. 

The recently single actress, 41, is "deeply and intensely loved by her friends," said Jessica Seinfeld, wife to Jerry Seinfeld and Paltrow inner-circle bestie. Seinfeld, 42, shared a cute photo via Instagram of herself with Paltrow on Sunday, Aug. 17, praising the star and spilling on her friendships. 

"I have never met anyone with more true and loyal best friends than this baby girl," Seinfeld captioned the shot of the two blonde buds embracing. "She is deeply and intensely loved by her friends. I hope each of you has someone in your life that is a wise and steady North Star like this one is to so many."

"Why not tag those people in your life who kill it in the friendship department?" she continued of Paltrow. "It's Show Appreciation Sunday. (I made that up)."

One of Paltrow's childhood friends, Julia Cuddihy Van Nice, chimed in on the photo, adding, "Ain't that the truth @jessseinfeld. Amen!"

The outpouring of support from Paltrow's friends comes just days after news broke that the Goop founder's estranged husband Martin, 37, may be dating Jennifer Lawrence. Martin, who with Paltrow announced the end of a 10-year marriage in March, is rumored to be spending time with the Hunger Games star, 24. 

But Paltrow herself is moving on from Martin as well: Us Weekly exclusively revealed last week that the Oscar winner is dating Glee co-creator Brad Falchuk. "Things started off professionally" between Paltrow and Falchuk, a source told Us, as the pair got to know one another when the actress made appearances on Glee.
