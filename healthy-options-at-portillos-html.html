Healthy Options At Portillos - Statcare

Are you a fan of Portillos but worried about finding healthy options on their menu? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll show you how to make smart choices at Portillos that will keep you on track with your health goals.

Why Choose Healthy Options?

Maintaining a balanced diet is essential for overall health and well-being. By opting for healthy choices, you can enjoy your favorite foods while still nourishing your body with the nutrients it needs.

Tips for Choosing Healthy Options

  • Look for grilled or baked options instead of fried ones. This will help reduce the amount of unhealthy fats in your meal.

  • Opt for lean protein sources like chicken or fish.

  • Load up on vegetables by choosing salads or adding extra veggies to your sandwich.

  • Choose whole grain options for added fiber and nutrients.

  • Be mindful of portion sizes and avoid supersizing your meal.

  • Skip sugary drinks and opt for water or unsweetened beverages instead.

  • Limit your intake of high-calorie condiments like mayo or creamy dressings.

  • Don't be afraid to ask for modifications or substitutions to make your meal healthier.

Healthy Options at Portillos

Now that you know the tips for choosing healthy options, let's dive into some specific choices you can make at Portillos:

1. Grilled Chicken Sandwich

  • Choose the grilled chicken breast option instead of the breaded and fried one.

  • Opt for a whole wheat bun for added fiber.

  • Load up on veggies like lettuce, tomato, and onion.

  • Skip the mayo and opt for mustard or a light dressing.

2. Chopped Salad

  • Choose the chopped salad with grilled chicken or shrimp.

  • Load up on colorful veggies like bell peppers, cucumbers, and carrots.

  • Ask for dressing on the side and use it sparingly.

3. Portobello Mushroom Sandwich

  • Opt for the Portobello mushroom sandwich instead of a meat-based one.

  • Choose a whole wheat bun for added fiber.

  • Load up on veggies like lettuce, tomato, and onion.

  • Skip the cheese or ask for a lighter cheese option.

4. Veggie Burger

  • Choose the veggie burger option instead of a meat-based one.

  • Load up on veggies like lettuce, tomato, and onion.

  • Skip the mayo and opt for mustard or a light dressing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any vegetarian options at Portillos?

Yes, Portillos offers several vegetarian options like the Portobello mushroom sandwich and the veggie burger.

Can I customize my order at Portillos?

Absolutely! Portillos is known for its customization options, so feel free to ask for modifications or substitutions to make your meal healthier.

Are there any low-carb options at Portillos?

Yes, you can opt for a lettuce wrap instead of a bun for any sandwich or burger at Portillos.

Conclusion

Eating healthy doesn't mean you have to give up your favorite foods. By making smart choices and opting for healthier options at Portillos, you can enjoy a delicious meal while still staying on track with your health goals. So next time you visit Portillos, remember these tips and make choices that will nourish your body and satisfy your taste buds!
