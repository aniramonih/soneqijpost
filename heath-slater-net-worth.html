What is Heath Slater's Net Worth?

Heath Slater is an American professional wrestler who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Heath Slater was born in Pineville, West Virginia in July 1983. He started his wrestling career in the World Wrestling Alliance from 2004 to 2006. Slater competed on the first season of WWE NXT in 2010 after wrestling in Deep South Wrestling from 2006 to 2007 and in Florida Championship Wrestling from 2007 to 2010.

Slater has been a member of the factions The Nexus, The Corre, and The Social Outcasts. He is a three time WWE Tag Team Champion with Justin Gabriel and won a Slammy Award for Shocker of the Year in 2010 for the debut of The Nexus. Slater held the FCW Florida Heavyweight Championship, Florida Tag Team Championship, and Southern Heavyweight Championship.
