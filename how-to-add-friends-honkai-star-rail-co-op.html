Star Rail &amp; is it multiplayer co-op?

Honkai: Star Rail is the latest game to come out that is developed by miHoYo. Similar to Genshin Impact, it also features a gacha system where you can pull characters and weapons to make them more powerful.

For those that are looking for a multiplayer experience, it may be a little bit confusing as to how to get started with co-oping in Honkai: Star Rail. This guide will explain how you can add friends in Honkai: Star Rail and whether the game is a multiplayer co-op title or not.

How to add friends in Honkai: Star Rail

If you are wanting to add friends in Honkai: Star Rail, there are two ways in which you can do so. These are the two ways to add friends in Honkai: Star Rail.

You can copy and paste their unique ID number by copying and pasting it or you can individually type each number in. Once you have done that, it will bring up the player’s profile, and you can send them a friend request.

Equally, if you want to add friends but don’t know who they are, you can add them from the Friends menu tab which you can then add as many as you like. You can also refresh the list and get a list of new people to add. You can have up to 50 people at a time. 

Is Honkai: Star Rail multiplayer co-op?

Honkai: Star Rail is not multiplayer in the traditional sense the way Genshin Impact is. Instead, you can use their support characters temporarily. However, you can only use the support system in Calyxes and Caverns of Corrosion. Other than that, you are completely on your own.

To be able to add a support member to your team, you go to the Calyxes or Caverns of Corrosion and press the green support button that is next to your fourth team member. Once you’ve done so, this will bring up the list of support members that you can borrow from their friend list.  

Then, you can select a character of your choice from the Support Character they will then join your team for the full duration of the Calyxes or Caverns of Corrosion. 

That’s everything you need to know about adding friends in Honkai: Star Rail and whether or not it is multiplayer co-op. For more information, keep reading The Games Cabin.
