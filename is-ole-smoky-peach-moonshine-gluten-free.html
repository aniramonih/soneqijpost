What is Gluten?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. It can cause serious health problems for people with celiac disease and gluten intolerance.

What is Celiac Disease?

Celiac disease is a genetic autoimmune disorder in which the ingestion of gluten leads to damage in the small intestine. It affects about 1 in 100 people worldwide.

What is Gluten Intolerance?

Gluten intolerance, also known as non-celiac gluten sensitivity, is a condition in which people experience symptoms similar to celiac disease when they consume gluten. However, they do not have the same intestinal damage as those with celiac disease.

What are the Symptoms of Celiac Disease and Gluten Intolerance?

Symptoms of celiac disease and gluten intolerance may include abdominal pain, bloating, diarrhea, constipation, fatigue, headache, joint pain, and skin rashes.

Are all Moonshine Products Gluten-Free?

No, not all moonshine products are gluten-free. It is important to check the ingredients list or contact the manufacturer to confirm whether a particular moonshine is gluten-free.
