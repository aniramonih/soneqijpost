Jeb Stuart Adams Biography

Jeb Stuart Adams is one of the most popular and richest Businessperson who was born on April 10, 1961 in Hollywood, California, United States.

Adams began his career at a young age. In 1969, he won a part in the film Run, Angel, Run. He also had a part in the film The Goonies. Arguably, his most memorable role was that of Christopher in the 1987 psychological horror film Flowers in the Attic. His television credits include Baa Baa Black Sheep, Quincy, M.E. and Airwolf.

In adulthood, Adams became a professional production designer in commercials, working such directors as Michael Cuesta and Tony Kaye. Adams is no longer an actor, his final acting role having been in the made-for-TV movie Sworn to Vengeance in 1993. He is now a successful realtor in Ventura County, California.

Jeb Stuart Adams (born April 10, 1961) is an American realtor and former actor. He comes from a family of successful actors, including his parents Nick Adams and Carol Nugent.
