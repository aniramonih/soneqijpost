Career

Dewan made her debut in Janet Jackson’s “Doesn’t Matter” clip in 2000, continued with “All for You” and the All for You Tour in 2001.

Partnering with Jackson benefited her professionally, enabling her to engage with a variety of other musicians later on and providing her with the expertise she needed to co-star in the dancing movie Step Up.

Dewan was the ambassador of Danskin dance-wear in a commercial and picture session in 2016 that highlighted and emphasized her agility as a performer. She subsequently collaborated with Christina Aguilera, Pink, and Missy Elliott, among many others.

Jenna made her acting debut in Tom Brady’s 2002 teen fantasy comedy film, The Hot Chick. She was cast to portray the role of Bianca Salsa Girl.

The film had featured a fabulous ensemble cast of Eric Christian Olsen, Rob Schneider, Rachel McAdams, Anna Faris, Matthew Lawrence. It generated $54.6 million at the box office. The film had a 22% approval rating and a 3.8/10 average rating on Rotten Tomatoes.
