Justin Bieber Roast: Comedians Viciously Mock Selena Gomez Romance

It looks like Selena Gomez is paying yet again for her relationship with Justin Bieber. On Saturday, March 14, the Biebs taped his highly anticipated Comedy Central Roast where no topic was off-limits, especially his on-again, off-again romance with “The Heart Wants What It Wants” singer, 22. 

“Selena Gomez wanted to be here, but she’s dating men now,” comedian Jeff Ross joked. “Is it true you dumped her because she grew a mustache before you?”

And that wasn’t even the most controversial comment from Ross, who also called Bieber, 21, “the King Joffrey of Pop.”

“Selena Gomez had sex with [you]… proving Mexicans will do the disgusting jobs Americans just won’t do,” Ross added. 

But not all comments were directed at Gomez. Naturally, the “Boyfriend” singer got the brunt of the attack.
