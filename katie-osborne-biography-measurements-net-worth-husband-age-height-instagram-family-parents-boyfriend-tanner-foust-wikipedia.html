Career

Katie Osborne is a sideline reporter for Fox Sports and has worked for other sports teams. She’s an interesting and successful woman in a male-dominated industry with a lot of great advice. 

Making it in sports broadcasting isn’t easy for anyone, but it’s especially difficult for women. The good news is that you can do a few things to increase your chances of success. First, be passionate about the sport you’re covering. Know the game inside and out, and talk about it intelligently.
