    Kevin Frazier Facts

  • While growing up, he wanted to follow in his father’s footsteps and become a basketball coach himself. Moreover, he served as an assistant coach to his father for 2 years in the 1980s.
  • Before turning to entertainment news, Kevin worked as a sportscaster and hosted numerous sports news programs on ESPN, Prime Sports, and Fox Sports Networks.
  • He grew up admiring the football team, Baltimore Colts (which is now the Indianapolis Colts).
  • In 2013, Frazier was signed on to host a game show pilot called The Money Pump, which was based on an Israeli show. The contestants on the show had to compete against an enormous money pump for the opportunity to win the $1 million prize. However, the show was not picked up beyond the pilot stage.
  • His appearances in television shows include 90210 (2010), The Millers (2014), Empire (2018), One on One (2005), Nobodies (2017), The Bold and the Beautiful (2020), Inside Schwartz (2001), etc.
  • Kevin has featured in small roles, usually playing himself, in films such as Rat Race (2001), Juwanna Mann (2002), Girls Trip (2017), etc.
  • He shares his birth date with many prominent celebrities such as Timothy Olyphant, Matt Czuchry, Tony Stewart, Austin McBroom, Cher, Ted Allen, Busta Rhymes, Rachel Platten, Josh O’Connor, Bronson Pinchot, Lacey Wallace, John Billingsley, Jon Pardi, Loey Lane,  and many others.
  • Kevin founded the urban entertainment website called HipHollywood.com in 2008.
