Who was Kevin Karlson?

Kevin Karlson was a prominent figure in the world of classic rock radio, best known for his role as a radio DJ and co-host of the morning show on 100.7 WZLX-FM in Boston. With a career spanning several decades, Kevin Karlson left an indelible mark on the radio industry, entertaining and connecting with his audience through his distinctive voice and witty banter.
