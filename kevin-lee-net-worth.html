What is Kevin Lee's Net Worth?

Kevin Lee is an American professional mixed martial artist who has a net worth of $400 thousand. Kevin Lee was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan in September 1992. He competed as a lightweight from 2012 and 2018 and as a welterweight starting in 2019. Lee made his professional MMA debut with a win over Levis Labrie in March 2012. In November 2013 he defeated Eric Moon to win the TWC Lightweight Championship.

Kevin Lee suffered his first loss against Al Iaquinta in February 2014 in his UFC debut. In November 2016 he had the Performance of the Night in a win over Magomed Mustafaev. Lee again had the Performance of the Night in a win over Michael Chiesa in June 2017. In October 2017 he lost to Tony Ferguson for the interim UFC Lightweight Championship. Kevin Lee made his welterweight debut with a loss to Rafael dos Anjos in May 2019 to bring his record to 17-5.
