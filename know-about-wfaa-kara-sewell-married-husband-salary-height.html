Know About WFAA Kara Sewell; Married, Husband, Salary, Height

Published Date: 07 Mar, 2019 Updated date: 07 Mar, 2019 By Celebs Podium

It is a matter of surprise that Kara Sewell and her husband has been together since the beginning of her career. The couple is risk takers, who broke the rule of their workplace at the beginning of their relationship.

Kara Sewell is an American journalist, who serves at ABC-affiliated channel WFAA in Dallas, Texas, joining in 2018. She currently appears as a co-anchor on WFAA’s Daybreak morning news.

Kara Sewell Bio; Birthday, Height, Education

Kara was born in Houston and her birthday falls on the 23rd of August every year. Keeping her age a mystery, she studied communication and media at the University of St. Thomas.

Later, she got graduated from the Stephen F. Austin State University with a bachelor degree in media studies. The charming lady stands at a height of 5 feet 4 inches (1.62 meters).

Journalism Career & Salary

After her graduation, Kara started her career as a weekend evening anchor at ICA broadcasting ILTD where she worked for two years until 2008.

Soon after, she joined CBS-affiliated KWCH-DT and worked as an anchor for half hour morning newscast and a 30-minute noon newscast five days a week. She worked in the station for more than 6 years and then joined FOX 19 in 2014.

Kara Sewell currently co-anchors daybreak morning news in WFAA (Picture: Kara’s Instagram)

While working on FOX19, Kara led coverage on site at both the Republican and Democratic national conventions in the 2016 American presidential campaign. She now serves as a daybreak co-anchor on WFAA, writing, producing and analyzing news stories on tight deadlines.

Kara has been involved in the journalism field for more than a decade. When it comes to earning, she obtains an average salary of $83000 from the media.

Together With Husband Since A Decade

Kara and her husband, Nick has been together for more than a decade. The couple exchanged their wedding vows on 7th October 2010 and has been inseparable since then.

They met as a coworker at ICA broadcasting ILTD and broke the unspoken rule about dating a coworker. She often share pictures with her husband on her social sites.

Kara Sewell and her husband Nick have been married for more than eight years (Picture: Kara’s Instagram)

Her husband Nick is a morning new editor, who previously worked as a photojournalist. The pair are the parents of a Pomeranian named Bailey.
