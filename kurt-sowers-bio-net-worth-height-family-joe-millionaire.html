How Much Is Kurt Sowers Net Worth? Bio, Height, Family, Joe Millionaire

Joe Millionaire: For Richer or Poorer featured Kurt Sowers and Steven McBee who were pursued by 20 eligible women looking to find love. But there’s a catch. Only one of the bachelors is a millionaire, the other hails from a modest family.

So, who’s Kurt? What’s his net worth and height? Stick with this Kurt Sowers Bio to find answers.

Kurt Sowers On Joe Millionaire

Kurt Sowers would do anything to find “the one” for him. He wants his future wife to fill his life with laughter. Thus, he appeared on Joe Millionaire: For Richer or Poorer to “find love in an unorthodox way.”

Talking about his view on relationships, Kurt shared “I don’t ever want to be apart from them. And when I am apart from them, I can’t wait to get back to them.” Also, he added that he was a “born competitor” and if he does something, he does it to win it.

Guess, he was set on finding love.

The contestants vying for Kurt’s heart this season were — Suzan, Amanda Pace, Calah Jackson, Breanna Hagen, Amber, Andreea Natalia, Caroline Campbell, Doris Josie Cano, Annie Jorgensen, Jennie Alexandra, Carolyn Moore, Brookell, Rachel Vinson, Monica Aksamit, Katy Johnson, Sara Rose, Suzette James, and two other.

Each one of them flaunts an amazing personality. No wonder, Kurt confessed that it was “very difficult” to eliminate them. “It was very difficult to say goodbye to them. Sometimes you felt like you weren’t even giving them a fair shot, even just because of the flow and the process,” he explained.

So, while the show may have its appeal, many viewers felt like the original version misled the women (which was partially true). But this time it’s different. “In this remake, there’s no lie being told whatsoever,” Steven shared.

“The women know exactly what’s going on. They know one of us is wealthier than the other, they just don’t know which is which,” he added.

As to answer if Kurt found love this season, we’ll just have to wait and see. But love or not, he formed an unbreakable bond with his co-star Steven, which is precious in itself.

Kurt Sowers Job: What Does He Do?

Kurt Sowers is the CEO of a development organization based in Charlotte, North Carolina. Also, he has stepped into the field of acting/modeling as well. The types of casting that interested him were — commercials, feature films, music videos, theater, comedy clowns, and extras.

More information about his job adventures and career was kept under wraps, to add mystery to Kurt’s real identity.

Seems like Kurt is a globe trotter.

Kurt Sowers Family

Kurt Sowers come from a big family. He was born to parents Colleen and Eric Sowers who lived in Weddington, North Carolina, and were very religious.

As for his siblings, Kurt has five brothers and sisters named Vanda, Tara, Kelly, Bret, and Sean. Among them, his sister Tara was happily married to Jeremy Langdale with 4 kids. She is also the owner of Miss Tara Belle and Vis-a-Vis.

Unfortunately, Kurt’s grandfathers June E. Eyman and Hugh H. “Jim” Sowers have both passed away. But he isn’t entirely sad about it. Explaining his nervousness about his family watching the show, he joked,  “I’m just glad my grandparents have all passed.”

Also, Kurt has aunts and uncles named —Gwen Jackson, Sally (Jim) Moreno, Faith Nussdorfer, and Daniel Sowers.
