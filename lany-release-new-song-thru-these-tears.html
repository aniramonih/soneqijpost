LANY Release New Song 'Thru These Tears'

LANY released their emotional new song, Thru These Tears, today. It is the first single that has been released from their upcoming album, Malibu Nights.

The Los Angeles-based trio, made up of Paul Klein, Les Priest and Jake Goss, had been teasing the new single for weeks as they close out the “rose era,” which is a nod to the artwork featured on their self-titled debut album that was released last summer.

The new single launches the “moon era,” which is a symbolic reference to LANY’s sophomore album Malibu Nights, which will be released later this year.

Thru These Tears premiered today on Beats 1 and frontman Paul Klein told Zane Lowe that Malibu Nights is “bigger than a break-up album” and “is just about being human.”
