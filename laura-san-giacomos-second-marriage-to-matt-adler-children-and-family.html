Laura San Giacomos Second Marriage To Matt Adler: Children And Family

Laura San Giacomo had given up on finding love after a failed marriage with Cameron Dye. Against all odds, Laura found love again in Matt Adler, with whom she has been married for almost 19 years.

Mason Dye, her son, is the proud mother of the 56-year-old. She shared a platform with him at UCPWheels to discuss those who require a wheelchair. If you want to learn more about her married connection and family, keep reading.

Laura San Giacomo’s Marriage to Matt Adler

The actress married Matt in 2000, two years after her first marriage ended in divorce. Torry Castellano’s cousin and well-known actor is Matt Adler. He appeared as a guest star in a number of TV shows.

In the late 1990s, appearing in an episode of Miami Vice paid off for Matt. He met Laura for the first time on the set of the show. Matt asked her out with some usual talk after the duo locked eyes.

Adler and Laura went on a date and thoroughly enjoyed each other’s company. They became a thing after a few dates and wanted to take their relationship to the next level. The couple married in 2000 and now live blissfully in San Fernando Valley, California.

However, the exact date of their wedding and how they celebrated their special day has not been revealed. Although it was a private celebration, we can assume that the couple’s close celebrity acquaintances attended. They have not had a kid in their nineteen years of marriage.

Children of Laura San Giacomo

On November 19, 1995, the 56-year-old actress gave birth to a son called Mason Dye. Mason, unfortunately, suffers from Cerebral Palsy, a disorder caused by brain injury. Dye suffers from reduced muscle coordination and other problems as a result of this.

Despite their limitations, Mason and Laura want to give back to their community. Laura took center stage in the UCPWheels program to discuss her son’s impairments. Mason, in his wheelchair, even took center stage.

In the show, San Giacomo discussed how 70 million individuals with disabilities worldwide require wheelchairs but do not have access to them. Dye also expressed his gratitude for his wheelchair.

Laura does not have any other children besides Mason.

Divorce of Laura San Giacomo from Cameron Dye

Laura was previously married to actor Cameron Dye from 1990 to 1998 before marrying Matt Adler. The couple most likely met on the set of the film and fell in love. Cameron Dye is the father of Mason Dye, who was born in 1995 with Laura.

After eight years of marriage, the couple divorced in 1998, however, the cause for the divorce is unknown. Laura is currently content with her marriage to Matt and spending time with her family.

Laura San Giacomo’s Personal and Family Life

San Giacomo spends a significant amount of time with her family. Aside from that, her career is flourishing, and she has appeared in numerous TV shows and films. Laura has wowed everyone since the beginning of her profession. Throughout her career, she has appeared opposite Michael Weatherly, Stana Katic, and many more.

The actress is involved in a number of charitable programs, particularly those aimed at people with impairments. Laura also enjoys horseback riding, gymnastics, ice skating, ballet, tennis, and playing the piano in her spare time. She has a huge net worth and resides in San Fernando with her family. Laura is doing an excellent job raising her child and has also discussed what it means to raise a special needs child.
