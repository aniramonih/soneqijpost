Who is in the cast of Law and Order: Criminal Intent?

LAW & ORDER: Criminal Intent was on the air from 2001 to 2011.

During that time, Law and Order fans got a look at the lives of the Major Case Squad detectives and their unconventional methods.

Who is in the cast of Law and Order: Criminal Intent?

The cast of Law and Order: Criminal Intent includes:

Kathryn Erbe

Kathryn Erbe portrayed the role of Detective Alexandra 'Alex' Eames from 2001-2011.

Outside of Law & Order, she is known for her role as Shirley Bellinger in the HBO series Oz.

Erbe was married to Terry Kinney from 1993 to 2006 and is the mother of daughter Maeve Elsbeth and son Carson Lincoln.

Vincent D'Onofrio

Vincent D'Onofrio portrayed the role of Detective Robert 'Bobby' Goren from 2001-2011.

Outside of Law & Order, he is known for his roles in Men in Black, The Cell, Full Metal Jacket, and Ed Wood.

Since 2019, he has also portrayed the role of Vincent "Chin" Gigante in the TV series Godfather of Harlem.

D'Onofrio is currently married to photographer Carin van der Donk and has three kids - Leila, Elias, and Luka.

Leslie Hendrix

Leslie Hendrix portrayed the role of Medical Examiner Dr Elizabeth Rodgers from 2001-2011.

Between 1992 and 2010, she also starred as Rodgers in over 100 episodes of the original Law & Order series.

Outside of Law & Order, she is known for her role as Judge Hannah Lampert on the soap opera All My Children and as Kathryn in the TV series Gotham.

Chris Noth

Chris Noth portrayed the role of Detective Michael 'Mike' Logan from 2005 to 2008.

Like Hendrix, he also starred as Logan in over 100 episodes of the original Law & Order series from 1990 to 1995.

Outside of Law & Order, he is famously known for his role as Mr Big in the Sex and the City franchise.

Since 2012, Noth has been married to Tara Wilson and is the father of sons Keats and Orion.

Jamey Sheridan

Jamey Sheridan portrayed the role of Captain James 'Jimmy' Deakins from 2001-2006.

Outside of Law & Order, is known for his roles in The Ice Storm, Syriana, Sully, and Spotlight.

Since 1992, he has been married to actress Colette Kilroy and is the father of son Quin Kalis Sheridan.

Jeff Goldblum

Jeff Goldblum portrayed the role of Detective Zack Nichols from 2009-2010.

Outside of Law & Order, he is famously known for his role as Ian Malcolm in the Jurassic Park franchise as well as his role as David Levinson in Independence Day.

Since 2014, he has been married to Emilie Livingston and is the father of sons Charlie Ocean and River Joe.

Courtney B Vance

Courtney B Vance portrayed the role of ADA Ronald 'Ron' Carver from 2001-2006.

Outside of Law & Order, he is known for his roles in The Hunt for Red October, Hamburger Hill, Dangerous Minds, Space Cowboys, and 61st Street.

Since 1997, he has been married to Angela Bassett and is the father of daughter Bronwyn and son Slater.

Eric Bogosian

Eric Bogosian portrayed the role of Captain Danny Ross from 2006-2010.

Outside of Law & Order, he is known for his roles in Talk Radio, Wonderland, Uncute Gems, Under Siege 2: Dark Territory, and Billions.

Since 1980, he has been married to theater director Jo Anne Bonney and is the father of sons Harris Wold and Travis.

How can I watch Law and Order: Criminal Intent?

Criminal Intent was first shown on NBC but during its seventh season, it switched to USA Network.

After three seasons on USA, the show was permanently canceled due to the cost.

"USA gave it every shot, at great cost. I wouldn't blame them [for not renewing]. Simply put, USA didn't make enough money on LOCI to justify even the final 8 they did. They felt they owed it to the fans," former showrunner Warren Leight said in 2013, via Looper.
