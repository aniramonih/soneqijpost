Love And Hip Hop Star Brittney Taylor Arrested for Allegedly Attacking the Father of Her Child


Reality TV personality Brittney Taylor, known for her role in “Love & Hip Hop: New York,” is facing legal trouble after being accused of physically assaulting the father of her children with a baseball bat.

According to security officials who spoke to TMZ, Brittney was involved in a physical altercation with Afeez, the father of her 2-year-old and 8-month-old children. She surrendered to authorities last week after a warrant was issued for her arrest.

The alleged incident occurred when Afeez traveled to New York to spend time with his children, despite having maintained some distance from Brittney due to their strained relationship.

The altercation reportedly took place after Brittney said something that Afeez chose to ignore while leaving the apartment with the children and his mother.

According to a criminal complaint obtained by TMZ, Afeez informed law enforcement that Brittney punched him in the face in front of their children. After leaving the residence, Afeez alleged that Brittney followed him, wielding a bat and striking him, causing a laceration and significant facial bleeding.

Afeez sought medical attention at a hospital, where he received stitches for a cut above his eye. Brittney has been charged with two counts of assault, one count of endangering the welfare of a child, and one count of criminal possession of a weapon.
