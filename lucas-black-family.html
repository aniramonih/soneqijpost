Lucas Black Bio & Family

The actor was born as Lucas York Black on 29 November 1982 in Decatur, Alabama, to parents, Jan Gillespie and Larry Black. Besides his parents, his other family includes his two older siblings named Lee and Lori Black. Lucas played football for the Speake Bobcats and graduated from high school in May 2001.
