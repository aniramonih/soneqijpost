Madison Bailey (born January 29, 1999) is an American Actress, Model, and Social Media Personality from North Carolina. She first received recognization in the entertainment industry with an NBC’s show “Constantine”.

The main casts of the Constantine are Matt Ryan, Angélica Celaya, Charles Halford, Harold Perrineau, etc. Madison rose to fame after starring in the 2020 Netflix program called “Outer Banks”.

Career, TV Shows & Films

The actress starred in her debut show “Constantine” at the age of 16. Later, in 2017, Madison Bailey worked in two more television series. She acted in two episodes of Murder Chose Me as Angela Henry.

Madison appeared in episodes called 101 Edwards and Deadliest Sin. The same year, she featured in the “Gods Who Fall” episode of Mr. Mercedes show as Chloe. The actress also made her pictorial debut with a 2017 film “Fist Fight” as High School Student.

Later, she played Jackie in her movie titled “Nightclub Secrets”. Her next TV show was Two Roads. Bailey also acted for one of the recurring roles as Wendy Hernandez in the CW series “Black Lightning”.

She played one of the important roles as Kiara in the Netflix show “Outer Banks”. It is an American teen drama web television program which was premiered on April 15, 2020. Kiara is one of the members of the Pogues which is led by John B (performed by Chase Stokes).

Valerie Weiss, Jonas Pate, and Cherie Nowlan worked as directors of the show. Moreover, it was created by Jonas Pate, Josh Pate, and Shannon Burke.

Some of the other main casts of Outer Banks are Jonathan Daviss as Pope, Madelyn Cline as Sarah Cameron, Austin North as Topper, Rudy Pankow, Charles Esten as Ward Cameron, Drew Starkey as Rafe, Chase Stokes as John B and much more.
