Makka Mohamed Salah is the star kid, famous as the daughter of legendary Egyptian player, Mohamed Salah, who is currently playing for Premier League club Liverpool, and the Egypt National Team.

Mohamed Salah, the Egyptian Messi

The outstanding player, Mohamed Salah, used to play for El Mokawloon and the Egyptian Premier League in his early youth days. 

Later, by representing the nation, he got the opportunity to grab a worldwide audience’s attention.

While playing internationally, his contribution made Egypt reach the final of the 2017 Africa Cup of Nation and 2018 FIFA World Cup.

He successfully got different titles such as CAF Most promising African Talent of the Year in 2012 summer Olympics and CAF African Player of the Year in 2017 and 2018 etc. 

Chelsea recognized his talent and signed him while he was playing for the Swiss club, Basel.

Creative and enthusiastic, Mohamed is nicknamed as ‘Egyptian Messi‘ by the Italian media for his technical skills, pace, left foot, goal-scoring, position, and direct playing style. 

His idol, Ronaldo, a retired Brazilian World cup Winner, also stated that he is an incredible player with tremendous quality and looks like Messi. 

Married to a childhood friend

The family man, Mohamed, is married to his childhood friend, Magi Salah. 

The couple has known each other since their early adolescent period and has been there for each other.

Their first meeting happened because of their parents as they were the teachers in their school, Mohamed Eyad AI-Tantawi. 

The couple got married when Mohamed had just started staying in Europe to play for the Swiss football club, Basel. 

During his first holiday, he returned to his village and tied the knot with his childhood friend cum love of his life.

Wedding in the presence of the myriad audience

The couple held their wedding function on December 13, 2013.

It was organized in their hometown village, Nagrig, traditionally.

As the couple wanted to make every member of the village a part of their big day, they exchange their wedding vows with the presence and blessing of a myriad audience of the village. 

As per Nagrig’s traditional customs, the villagers gifted the couple practical gifts that would help them set up their marriage.

Egyptian singer and actor Hamada Hilal was also there to bless the newlyweds.

Singers Al Basit Hamouda and Saad AI Sughayar alsoperformed for the newlyweds.

Wife handling the charity affairs 

Although his wife prefers to live a simple life far away from the limelight world and media, she cannot shun it while supporting her husband in the stadiums and different celebrations. 

Apart from being a celebrity spouse, she has her own identity. 

She is a graduate of biotechnology from Alexandria University. 

Currently, she is handling the charity affairs of her husband in Egypt.

Her husband has a regeneration project in his hometown, where more than half the population lives in poverty. 

His donation is working to build schools and hospitals there. 

The adorable couple has also nourished more than 450 families by giving monthly allowances. 

As he is busy playing, her wife regularly attends major events of their hometown village to contribute as much as possible. 

She is also seen helping the newlyweds’ couple in buying the necessary home appliances and other needs.

Parents are devout Muslims

Makka’s parents are the devout Muslims who like to maintain their connections to their rural roots.

The couple returns to their hometown in every Ramadan to shares their wealth among the villagers.
