Malachy Murphy Is Cillian Murphy's First Son: Here's All You Need To Know About Him

Malachy Murphy Birthday

Malachy Murphy Age 

Since he was born on December 4, 2005, Malachy is now 17 years old. He will clock 18 by December 2023.

As a father, the talented actor hopes his son can live confidently and happily regardless of his chosen profession.

Malachy Murphy Instagram 

Malachy Murphy is now old enough to run his own Instagram page, but like his father, who is known to keep his life private, Malachy is not active on the app. Cillian is a very private person known to prefer to give a handshake to a fan rather than take a photo with them.

Malachy Murphy Parents

Malachy was born to actor Cillian Murphy and his wife, Yvonne McGuinness. Malachy’s parents have been together for more than 26 years and have been married for nearly two decades. Cillian Murphy was born on May 25, 1976, in Douglas County, Cork, Ireland. Cillian Murphy and Malachy Murphy

Malachy Murphy Now
