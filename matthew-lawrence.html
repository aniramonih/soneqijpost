Matthew Lawrence Biography and Wiki

Matthew Lawrence born Matthew William Lawrence is a well-known American singer and actor. He is well-known for his roles in Mrs. Doubtfire and Boy Meets World. He also appeared in the television series Brotherly Love alongside his real-life brothers Joey and Andrew.

Matthew Lawrence Career

Lawrence made his acting debut in the mid-1980s, playing Danny Carrington in Dynasty. He studied acting at New York City’s HB Studio. Throughout the early 1990s, he worked as a child actor, appearing in a variety of television and feature film roles, including the 1993 Robin Williams comedy Mrs. Doubtfire and the mid-1990s television series Superhuman Samurai Syber-Squad. In addition, he continued to star in Brotherly Love, which starred his real-life brothers, and in Boy Meets World, where he played Jack Hunter, replacing Eric Matthews’ (Played by Will Friedle) old best friend Jason Marsden after Jason Marsden left the series after Season 2 was completed.
