Mauricio Ochmann Wife, Parents, Biography, Quick Facts

Mexican-American actor Mauricio Ochmann is a popular face in telenovelas. He hit the limelight with his appearance in Telemundo novels like Victorinos and El Clon in which he gave outstanding performances. Find out everything there is to know about the biography, parents and marriage of this movie star in this article.

Mauricio Ochmann Bio

The dashing actor was born in Washington DC USA on November 16, 1977. His love for acting is said to have started at a young age, but it was during his teenage years that he landed his first acting role in a television show La Otra Osa. He then moved to Los Angeles to study acting at Joanne Baron Studio and in the end he was given a minor role in the American film Message in a Bottle where he played the role of a postman. His professional play started in 1998 when he played the role of lead actor named Santiago Berriozabal in the Mexican film Azul Tequila and the following year he also landed another role in the film Háblame de amor

Over the years, the actor was able to exert a certain influence not only in American or Mexican films, but also in Spanish films. His films include the 2003 Ladies’ Evening, A la mala released in 2015 and Hazlo como hombre in 2017. Among the long list of television series that Mauricio Ochmann had presented are series on the big screen like Como en el cine in which he starred the main role of Javier, he was also in the series Miranda de mujer el regreso , Amarte asi Frijolito, Marina and Chocolat Dame. However, the actor would have stopped playing in the seriesMarina and Chocolat Dame due to some addiction issues he was battling with. Mauricio Ochmann was in the series Rosa diamante between 2012 and 2013 then moved to the series El Senor de los Cielos in which he played many roles which earned him a Telemundo award in 2015 for best bad boy. He played the male role in the movie El Chema as Jose Maria which aired from 2016 to 2017. Mauricio Ochmann is also known for his appearance in the sitcom Easy released in 2016 where he played the role of Martin.

Who are his parents?

The telenovela star didn’t have the privilege of being raised and not meeting his biological parents as he was adopted by a couple named Maria and Guillermo, but the couple divorced along the way, which led to mother Maria adopted by Ochmann, to move with him to Mexico. Later, she started a new relationship with a German named Thomas Ochmann, whose surname the actor currently bears. He was reportedly raised by Maria alongside her other children, Thomas, Christian and Paulo.

Mauricio Ochmann’s wife

Mauricio Ochmann is the husband of Mexican model and actress Aislinn Derbez with whom he starred in the film A La Mala in which he played the role of Santiago. The couple tied the knot on May 28, 2016 in a wedding ceremony held in Mexico. They have a beautiful daughter named Kailani which is a unique Hawaiian name that the couple chose to remember the amazing trip to Hawaii they took when their beautiful daughter was born, as Aislinn revealed on her Instagram page.

Prior to his current marriage, the talented actor was married to Maria Jose del Valle Prieto in 2008 and they have a daughter named Lorenza Ochmann. The couple split privately as no details or reasons were released as the cause of their divorce.
