Sportscaster Joe Buck and Reporter Michelle Beisner's wedding in Mexico! Also know about their Child

NFL host Joe Buck's wedding in Mexico grabbed some attention after his old-time friend Paul Rudd joined the party. He exchanged his wedding vows with reporter Michelle Beisner who are a match made in heaven.

In an interview with The Howard Stern Show, Joe also let some details about his wife cheerleading chaps. The couple cherishes every moment they can in their married life. Want to more details about their wedding and family life? Then, keep reading.

A Beachside Wedding...

Joe and Michelle arrived in Mexico with their family for the big day. Following them, all the high profile guests of Sports personalities and Hollywood Stars arrived to give the beautiful couple the blessing.

On April 12, 2014, the couple exchanged wedding vows in a beachside ceremony. In the breathtaking location of Cabo San Lucas, Mexico, stars like Paul Rudd, Troy Aikman, Tony Romo, Jerry Jones and his son Jerry Jones Jr. to name a few joined the party.

The pair stays silent on their nuptials as they have not shared any pics relating to their big day. As reported by TMZ, the couple got engaged in August 2013 after their six months of dating. 

Joe Buck on celebrating Fatherhood...

Prior to tying the knot with Michelle, Buck celebrated his eighteen-year married relationship with Ann Archambault. He and Ann carried on their nuptials in 1993.

In those glorious times, the couple experienced many ups and downs. One of the beautiful moments in the pairs' life is the arrival of their daughters. Ann gave birth to lovely Natalie Buck and Trudy Buck, both of them are now looking to start their career in front of the camera. 

In an interview with dailynews.com, Buck confirmed that Natalie's got the talent to be in the field of sports broadcasting. Natalie admires her stepmom Michelle and her profession but is not obsessed with it. As of now, there are no reports suggesting if she stepped into the field of sports broadcasting or somewhere else. 

Just like her sister, Trudy also wants to be in the showbiz industry. Buck said in an interview that she might be in the camera making of Jurassic World 3.

Michelle Beisner Thrill of being a Mother!

Beisner never had the thrill of being a mother until April 2018. In an interview with dailynews.com, Joe said that being a parent is not in our control. He loves raising kids and thinks that it is the most important job in the world. 

In April 2018, the Buck family expanded their family with the addition of two more children. The couple celebrated a double dose of joy by welcoming twin boys. In the recent photo shared with the US Magazine, the pair is hugging Wyatt Joseph and Blake Andrew in the hospital after their birth.
