Osmariel Villalobos Bio (Wiki)

Who is Osmariel Villalobos?

Osmariel Villalobos is a Venezuelan TV show host and model. She became Miss Earth Venezuela in 2011.

She also represented Venezuela in Miss Earth 2012 in the Philippines and won the “Miss Earth-Water” (2nd runner up) title and also Miss Photogenic Awards.

Osmariel Villalobos: Age, Parent, Ethnicity

Osmariel Villalobos was born on 02 August 1988 in Maracaibo, Venezuela. She is 33 years old. Her father’s name is Oswaldo Villalobos and her mother’s name is Mariela Atencio.

Osmariel Villalobos holds Venezuelan nationality and is of Latina ethnicity. Her siblings’ names are Osmarie Villalobos Varela, Oswaldo Villalobos Atencio, Oswaldo Villalobos Nava, and Oswaldo Villalobos Varela.

In which school did Osmariel Villalobos attend?

Osmariel Villalobos studied journalism, communication, and also audio-visual at the University of Zulia.
