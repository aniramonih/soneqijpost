Piper Rockelle Back on the Dating Scene with a New Boyfriend

Rising YouTube star Piper Rockelle got back into the dating scene almost a year after she parted ways with her ex boyfriend, Gavin Magnus. Presently, she is dating fellow YouTuber Lev Cameron.

Since confirming their relationship, the duo has been making numerous content with each other and has given glimpses of their relationship through YouTube videos.

Piper Rockelle and Lev Cameron's Relationship

Rockelle started dating Cameron on May 13, 2020. However, she only confirmed her relationship with him on July 18, 2020.

The Georgia native shared a picture with her boyfriend on Instagram alongside a caption that revealed when they officially began dating. 

Since the confirmation, the YouTuber duo has often appeared together in each other's social media feeds, sharing their mutual affection towards each other.

For instance, on December 13, 2020, Rockelle shared a picture on Instagram to celebrate their seven-month anniversary. In the post's caption, she expressed, "You are the [key] to my [heart]. 7 months check!"

Moreover, on 2021 Christmas, she uploaded a picture with her beau where she could be seen hugging her partner in front of a decorated Christmas tree. In the caption, she stated out loud that her boyfriend was the greatest gift of all. 

Interestingly, the feeling is mutual between the duo. Cameron also timely shares Rickelle's picture on his socials. On November 26, 2021, he posted a picture with his partner, writing he was thankful for her.

And a month before that,  the duo had dressed up as Toy Story characters for Halloween.

Piper Rockelle Loves Pranking Her Boyfriend

Love and trust are the key components for any relationship to flourish. But sometimes, fun is equally essential to a relationship, as is the case with the YouTube lovebirds.

The creator of The Squad, a group of content creators, is usually seen trying various pranks on her boyfriend on her YouTube channel. But, like their love, the pranks are also not one-sided, as Cameron also plays quite a few pranks on his girlfriend.

On November 26, 2020, she uploaded a YouTube video where she created a panic attack prank on her boyfriend. Likewise, in another instance, she performed a sleepwalking prank on him.

Piper Rockelle's Past Relationship with Gavin Magnus

Previously, Rockelle was in a dating relationship with YouTuber Gavin Magnus.

They were together for a long time; they used to do collaboration videos and feature each other on their respective YouTube channels. Besides, they even had an acronym for their relationship, Pavin, made from their first names.

Sadly, their togetherness didn't last — on a YouTube video published on July 24, 2019, Magnus revealed that he and Rockelle were no longer dating.

In another YouTube clip titled 'The TRUTH about our Break Up... (Not Clickbait) ft. Piper Rockelle,' released on November 24, 2019, Magnus opened up about the reason behind their break up.

As per his accounts, their relationship had become abusive and obsessive, due to which he broke up with her.
