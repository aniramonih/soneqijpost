The Pit of Eternal Sands

Within Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown, the Pit of Eternal Sands emerges as an exhilarating highlight, concealing a multitude of challenges for players to conquer. The Darkest of Souls quest introduces a puzzle, tasking players with unveiling a hidden chest within this perilous expanse. Yet, a word of caution is warranted, as the Pit teems with perilous foes and environmental hindrances.

Solving the Puzzle

To solve the puzzle in the Pit of Eternal Sands, players must follow a series of steps:

  • Demolish Giant Seals: Advance through the Pit by obliterating the giant seals. This progression will guide you to a site featuring a Wak-Wak Tree and Homa Statue on the right side, offering a convenient fast travel option.
  • Navigate the Southwest Path: Move southwest to a chamber adorned with imposing statues and a central platform. Descend through this platform as Sargon, the protagonist, to access a small alcove below.
  • Inspect Trap Mechanism: Within the confined alcove, discover two statues resembling adversaries from the Upper City. Utilize the Dimensional Claw to capture one of these foes.
  • Return to Puzzle Area: Return to the puzzle zone and toss the captured enemy. This action will activate trap pillars on both the left and right sides.
  • Vanquish the Adversary: Once the enemy succumbs to the trap pillars, a treasure chest emerges as a gratifying reward for successfully unraveling the puzzle.
  • Reclaim Your Reward: Sargon reaps the benefits with a Soma Tree Petal as a prize for deciphering the puzzle. This petal stands as a vital ingredient for augmenting his maximum health.

Gameplay and Features

In Prince of Persia: The Lost Crown, players take command of Sargon, a character endowed with a range of abilities including jumping, sliding, and air dashing, essential for maneuvering through various platforms. Armed with a pair of blades, Sargon engages enemies with precision, employing a parrying technique to accumulate Athra’s Glow, a special ability. The game introduces time-based powers for combat and movement, allowing Sargon to instantly dash through time or establish temporary checkpoints.

Exploration plays a pivotal role in The Lost Crown, as players traverse a seamlessly connected game world replete with shortcuts and concealed chambers. Progressing through the game involves solving puzzles and uncovering Time crystals, which contribute to enhancing Sargon’s arsenal. Furthermore, players have the option to capture and mark screenshots on the map, aiding in recalling specific locations for later return and puzzle-solving endeavors.
