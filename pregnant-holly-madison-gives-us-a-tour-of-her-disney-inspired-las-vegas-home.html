Inside Holly Madison's Disney-Inspired Las Vegas Home

She continued to show us a few of her favorite rooms, including the kitchen.

"This is one of my favorite rooms in the house, it's the kitchen. I always wanted a white and red 1940s style kitchen," she says. "Here's my refrigerator. I try to keep it pretty healthy at home. A lot of juices, and greens, green juice…even though being pregnant, green juice is so nauseating."

LOL, hey, at least she's honest.

Madison then led us to a room that seemed right out of your favorite fairy tale. "The main room here—it's kind of like my Beauty and the Beast library. I always wanted floor-to-ceiling book shelves, and we added these little hideaway nooks that we copied from the Adventureland suite in the Disneyland hotel."

Her home also included an indoor pool, which she explains is "handy" for Rainbow during the winter, an outdoor pool and a farm! "I love it back here. I love to spend a lot of time writing or just hanging out."
