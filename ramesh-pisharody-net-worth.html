Ramesh Pisharody Full Overview and Wiki

A Multifaceted Career Journey

Born on 1 October 1981, Ramesh Pisharody‘s journey in the entertainment world began early, with his first mimicry performance in school.

His talent for mimicry and observational comedy led him to the Cochin Stallions, a troupe founded by actor Salim Kumar, marking the start of his professional career.

Breaking into Television and Films

His versatility soon saw him transitioning to television, making a notable debut alongside Salim Kumar in an Onam-special program on Asianet in 2000.

His natural flair for comedy and engaging presence led to roles in several successful shows, including Cinemala and Bluffmasters, laying the foundation for his popularity.

The Film Industry and Directorial Success
