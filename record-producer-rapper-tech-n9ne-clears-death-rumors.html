Tech N9ne Bio, Wife & Net Worth

Tech N9ne was born on 8 November 1971 in Kansas City, Missouri as Aaron Dontez Yates to parents, Carlton Cook and Maude Sue Yates-Khalifah. His father had a job of Police Department (LAPD) and was estranged from the family. So he was brought up his mom who had epilepsy and lupus since she was 18 in a very strict Christian ancestry. Likewise, when Tech was 12 years of age Maude married for the second time with a Muslim named Abdul Hassan. The rapper has two half-brothers, Hakim Khalifah and Kiraameen Khalifah, and one half-sister, Basimah Khalifah, from his mother’s second marriage.

Speaking about his personal life, he married his girlfriend, Lecoya LeJuene in 1995. The former has two daughters named, Alyia and Reign Yates; aspiring singers. After a decade old matrimony, the pair started living separately since 2005. Almost ten years later, Tech went to the courts for divorce in 2015 which was only permitted in November 2017.

The 47-year-old has a net worth of $16 million from his musical career since 1991. All the credits of his high wealth are from studio albums like The Worst (2000), K.O.D (2009) and Strange Reign (2017).
