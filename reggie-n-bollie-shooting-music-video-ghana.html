Reggie 'N' Bollie Shooting Music Video in Ghana

X Factor 2015 runners up Reggie ‘N’ Bollie are heading home to Ghana to shoot their debut music video.

The lovable rap duo, who won the hearts of the nation during their time on The X Factor last year, are flying back to their homeland to shoot the music video for their upcoming debut single.

Since coming in second on X Factor 2015, Reggie ‘N’ Bollie have been signed to Simon Cowell’s record label, Syco, and have been touring the UK, building up a strong fan base.

The pair announced on Twitter that they are flying back to Ghana to shoot the music video soon, writing: “Ghana We’re coming home! We’re going to be shooting our debut music video in our home town & we’re so excited!” (sic).

If that wasn’t exciting enough, Reggie ‘N’ Bollie are also giving fans the chance to join them in Ghana and have the opportunity to star in the video.  Writing on Twitter, the boys said: “We would love to see you guys in Ghana be a part of our video! We will be sharing more details and dates soon!” (sic).

Reggie ‘N’ Bollie will also be guests on this week’s Celebrity Juice show. Catch them having fun with Keith Lemon this Thursday (21st April).
