Riann Steele's Boyfriend Gives Her a Reason to Smile

English actress Riann Steele has a special someone in her life who makes her laugh and look forward to tomorrow’s adventures. 

Steele, who plays Finola Jones in NBC’s Debris, is dating author Cebo Campbell. Though the adorable duo is low key about their personal and dating life, they provide occasional glimpses of their blissful relationship via their Instagram handles.

On December 22, 2019, Steele posted a romantic black-and-white photo with her man on her Instagram. In the picture, the actress gazes at the camera flaunting her dimples.

Beside Steele is her beau Campbell, with a hat on his head and a drink in his hand. Campbell, longingly, looks at his ladylove. 

Steele captioned the romantic photo with a long hearty message wherein she opened up about her difficult time.

The English actress went on further to address Campbell as someone who makes her smile.

Truth be told, these are some of the hardest months for me. Maybe it’s the pressure to be joyful, to consume everything in sight, spend hundreds of dollars on ‘stuff’ because ‘stuff’ is obviously the root to happiness.  But I do know, for me, family and laughter and human connections are what get me through. Grateful for you @cebocampbell for always giving me a reason to smile.

On April 13, 2020, the actress posted another picture with her beau on Instagram.

The picture was a selfie taken by Steele. Wearing a mask on their face, the duo seemed ready to fight the battle against COVID-19.

Not only Steele but Campbell was also seen posting about their bond on his Instagram handle. On June 29, 2019, the actress’s boyfriend posted an intimate picture as they held hands and leaned on to share a kiss.

The author mentioned how the photo was a throwback when he and Steele visited Bangkok together. It turns out Campbell was revisiting Bangkok, but this time without Steele by his side. He said:

A shot from earlier this year in #bangkok when I had this one to keep me company. Headed back on a solo, 24 hour flight to one of my favorite cities without my favorite person. Taking her good headphones though.

Looks like the duo's relationship is getting stronger with time.
