Roberto Bautista Agut is a Spanish professional tennis player who achieved his world No. 9 ranking in singles in November 2019 and has won several tournaments such as Dubai Tennis Championship, Auckland Open, Austrian Open Kitzbühel, and Rosmalen Championship. He has also participated in a variety of other tournaments such as Shanghai Masters, Wimbledon Championship, Heineken Open, St. Petersburg Open, Madrid Masters, Topshelf Open, Aircel Chennai Open, Croatia Open, US Open, Garanti Koza Sofia Open, Cincinnati Masters, and many more.
