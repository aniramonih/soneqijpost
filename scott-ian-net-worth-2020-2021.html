Career

After finding Anthrax in 1981, Scott Ian released the album ‘Fistful of Metal’ in 1984. The following year, the band came up with the album, ‘Spreading the Disease.’ Their third album ‘State of Euphoria’ was released in 1988.

The band released ‘Worship Music’ in 2011. Their most recent studio album, ‘For All Kings’ was released in 2016. The band has also released five live albums, seven compilation albums, six video albums, and 4 EPs.

Some of their popular singles include “Only,” “Black Lodge,” “The Devil You Know,” “Neon Nights,” “Breathing Lightning,” and “Ball of Confusion.” They have also released many music videos. He has also contributed to three of their videos.

Additionally, he has collaborated with other artists like Public Enemy, Brian Posehn, Apocalypse, Necro, and Pearl.
