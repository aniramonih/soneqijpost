Shannon Kelley family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Shannon Kelley is a businessman and former American football quarterback. He was recognized for his noteworthy career in both athletics and business.

Kelley’s football career began in high school at Houston Memorial High School, where he was a Class 5A All-State quarterback. This early achievement helped set the road for his future in sports.

Kelley began his college football career as the starting quarterback for the Texas Longhorns in 1988. Although he saw limited action in the seasons preceding 1987, he truly made his mark that year. Kelley exhibited his leadership and resilience on the field after filling in for Bret Stafford. Notably, he assisted in a comeback against BYU, and he scored his first touchdown with an outstanding 18-yard run against Oregon State.

Kelley had to fill in for an injured Stafford on several occasions this season, including his first career start in a 24-21 victory over TCU. Despite their ups and downs, the Longhorns concluded the season with a 6-5 record.

Kelley delved into other spheres of life after completing his undergraduate degree. He married Olympic champion and famed American gymnast Mary Lou Retton. Later, he moved into business, demonstrating his agility and adaptability.

Kelley later returned to his football roots by becoming a college coach. He worked as an assistant head football coach at Houston Christian University, where he shared his knowledge and passion for the game.
