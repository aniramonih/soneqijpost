Biography

Sidwell Sipho Ngwenya (born July 25, 1982), is a famous South African actor and musician widely known to his fans as Psyfo.

He was born on July 25, 1982, and he grew up in Kimberley, Northern Cape, South Africa.

He became a trending figure due to the role he played as Ajax in SABC1 soapie, Generations, between 2006 and 2010.

Early Life

Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya was born in Kimberley, Northern Cape, on the 25th of July 1982.

He relocated with his siblings to Johannesburg when he was still young.

This made it necessary for him to continue his education at Johannesburg.

Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya started his schooling at Zingisa Primary, a Xhosa school in Galeshewe. He relocated with his siblings to Johannesburg, where he attended Rivonia Primary School to compete for his primary school education.

To proceed with his study, he attended an audition held at the National School of the Arts.

Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya was auditioned for acting and fine arts, and he was accepted to do both. He was, however, required to major in one, which made him pick drama.

Career

Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya made his first appearance on local television after taking the role of a presenter and one of the lead singers on Kideo Pops. Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya started to become known quickly, which opened more doors for him as a presenter.

He was one of the lucky ones to be at Star Search. The show hunts for young talents – dancers, actors, and musicians- in South Africa (1996 until 2000).

In 2001, he graduated with fundamental skills and experience in Drama. However, immediately after his arrival, he chose to take a year off for an internship at A&P Film and Video. He arrived with the proper understanding of “happening behind the scene” of TV production. So he became the production manager of Sandra op ‘n Drafstap.

Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya had his most significant ever achievement on television in 2016 after getting the chance to play the role of Ajax on Generations. Generations are one of the most popular soap operas in the country. This character he played made many people love him and desired to see him more in other movies.

In 2013, Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya had an acting role. He played the role of acting as Sandile Nkambule in Rockville, which showed on the Mzansi Magic drama series.

Personal Life

Sipho Ngwenya, popularly known as Pysfo, who has preferred to stay single for reasons known to him, has finally decided to get married. So he proposed to the love of her life and wife to be Aamirah Mirah.

As a public figure, he took off to his social media page to announce the flourishing proposal scene and how he was accepted by Aamirah Mirah, “his girlfriend.”

After the positive response he got, they both went on holiday to Zanzibar, celebrating Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya’s birthday on July 25, 2020.

They officially got married on 21 November 2020 after over two years of dating.

Discography

In 2004, Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya decided to explore more by making use of his singing talent. His passion led him to an Opera singer June Kraus from who he got his training.

He got the chance to sign a year deal with the label Sting Music. He dropped his first single titled Don’t Be Scared under this label.

After this, Sipho ‘Psyfo’ Ngwenya’s first album shook the stores in 2005. In this unique album, he featured prominent local artists such as MXO, Skwatta Kamp and ProVerb.
