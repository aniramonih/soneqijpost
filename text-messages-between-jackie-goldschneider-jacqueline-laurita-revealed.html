Shocking Text Messages Between Jackie Goldschneider And Jacqueline Laurita Revealed

Credit: John Lamparski/Getty Images

Caught red handed? 

Jackie Goldschneider forwarded text messages between her and Jacqueline Laurita, to Melissa Gorga. The text messages throw some harsh words towards Teresa Giudice. 

Melissa read one message during the Real Housewives of New Jersey reunion part two, but more messages have been revealed. 

Instagram user @allabouttrh shared screenshots of text messages to their page. The text messages are allegedly between Jackie Goldschneider and Jacqueline Laurita from many years ago.

Jackie sent the text conversation to Melissa Gorga to bring up about the reunion.

“These texts were years ago and Jackie Goldschneider gave them to Melissa Gorga to bring up at the reunion even though this was years before Jacqueline and Teresa made up,” allabouttrh wrote.

“Jacqueline only spoke out on Teresa because she was getting blown up by Jackie Goldschneider and they weren’t in a good place,” they said. “Teresa and Jacqueline are better than ever and have let go of the past.”

Jackie’s intent was to get Teresa to turn on Jacqueline, despite them rekindling their friendship.

However, Jackie was the one to reach out first.

“Hi honey, It’s Jackie. Just wanted to make sure you have my cell number!” the reality star wrote to Jacqueline.

She then went on to say her manager monitors her Instagram account. She said that he is friends with the other RHONJ cast members so she wanted to keep their conversation private.

The series of text messages are cropped, however the two are talking about Teresa Giudice.
