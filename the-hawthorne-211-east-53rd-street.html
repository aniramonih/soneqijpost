Description of The Hawthorne at 211 East 53rd Street

211 East 53rd Street is a condo with 145 apartments, located in Midtown East very close to the M, 6 and E subway lines.

During the past two years, 17 apartments have sold: the most expensive was a two bedroom that sold for $1,875,000 (about $1,415 per sq. ft.), and the least expensive was a six plus bedroom that sold for $400,000.

Amenities at this pet friendly building include doorman, fitness center, rooftop terrace and full service garage.
