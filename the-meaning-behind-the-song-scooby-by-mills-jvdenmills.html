The Meaning Behind The Song: “SCOOBY” by Mills (jvdenmills)

TitleSCOOBY
ArtistMills (jvdenmills)
Writer/ComposerMills (jvdenmills)
AlbumM1*
Release DateSeptember 4, 2023
GenreRap
ProducerOhMyGon! & Mirandx

When I first heard the song “SCOOBY” by Mills (jvdenmills), I was immediately captivated by its infectious beat and catchy lyrics. As I delved deeper into the song, I discovered hidden meanings and personal connections that made me appreciate it even more.

The song starts with a catchy intro, setting the tone for what’s to come. The chorus, “Woke up and ran up the bag, I got a hoe and she ten out of ten,” depicts the artist’s confidence and success. It’s a celebration of accomplishments and having a perfect partner by their side. The lyrics talk about indulging in pleasures like dancing and smoking doobies, reflecting a carefree and party-filled lifestyle.

In Verse 1, Mills references his luxury car, the Wraith, and flaunts his affluent lifestyle. He talks about a foreign woman who accompanies him on private jets and satisfies his desires. The reference to her giving BJ’s underlines the explicit nature of the song, typical of the rap genre. Mills exudes self-assurance, emphasizing his ability to attract multiple women and enjoy his success.

Switching gears to Verse 2, Mills compares himself to Choppa, a rising rapper. He mentions taking a rich woman out to Red Lobster and hints at financial opportunities coming his way. The lyrics also touch upon blocking an ex who tries to reconnect, emphasizing the artist’s determination to move forward and focus on his own growth.

As I listened to “SCOOBY” repeatedly, the song started to resonate with me on a personal level. It reminded me of moments when I achieved something significant and felt on top of the world. It’s a song that exudes confidence and encourages listeners to embrace their own victories, no matter how big or small.

The lyrics, while seemingly superficial at first glance, also reflect the artist’s pursuit of personal fulfillment. Mills revels in the materialistic pleasures of his lifestyle, but the song hints at an underlying desire for purpose and meaning. As he mentions his mother being proud of his financial independence, it becomes clear that Mills seeks validation and approval from important figures in his life.

The production of “SCOOBY” deserves special mention. OhMyGon! and Mirandx, the producers of the song, have created a captivating beat that perfectly complements Mills’ energetic delivery. The combination of hard-hitting drums, melodic elements, and catchy hooks makes “SCOOBY” an irresistible track.

While some may argue that the lyrics of “SCOOBY” don’t delve deep into complex themes or offer profound insights, the song’s appeal lies in its ability to uplift and entertain. It’s a track meant to be enjoyed in the moment, to let loose and celebrate success, regardless of its depth or introspection.

In conclusion, the song “SCOOBY” by Mills (jvdenmills) is a catchy rap track that combines infectious beats with confident lyrics. It’s a celebration of success, material pleasures, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment. While it may not offer profound insights, it serves as a reminder to embrace our victories and enjoy life’s pleasures. So, turn up the volume and let “SCOOBY” take you on a ride filled with energy and self-assurance.
