The Meaning Behind The Song: Walk Like an Egyptian by The Bangles

Song Details:

TitleWalk Like an Egyptian
ArtistThe Bangles
Writer/ComposerLiam Sternberg
AlbumDifferent Light (1986)
Release DateJanuary 2, 1986
GenrePop-Rock
ProducerDavid Kahne

Walk Like an Egyptian by The Bangles was a smash hit in 1986, reaching number one on the charts and becoming Billboard’s top song of 1987. The song’s catchy melody and playful lyrics instantly captivated listeners worldwide. As a fan of The Bangles, this song holds a special place in my heart.

Upon listening to the lyrics, it becomes clear that Walk Like an Egyptian is not just about imitating the iconic Egyptian walk. It is a metaphor for breaking free from societal norms and expressing oneself without fear of judgment. The song takes us on a journey through various characters who are tired of conforming to the expectations placed upon them.

The opening verse talks about ancient Egyptian paintings on tombs and how they do the “sand dance.” This dance symbolizes breaking free from the constraints of tradition and embracing one’s own unique rhythm. The lyrics emphasize that those who move too quickly or dare to be different are often met with resistance, much like a falling domino.

The chorus, “Walk like an Egyptian,” is a call to action, encouraging listeners to embrace their individuality and walk confidently in their own path. It serves as a reminder to not be afraid of being different and to find joy in expressing oneself freely.

The second verse introduces the blonde waitresses who confidently spin around and cross the floor. Their moves are smooth and effortless, captivating everyone around them. This verse suggests that by embracing their true selves, these waitresses are able to bring joy and positivity to others. It’s a reminder that when we let go of societal expectations and embrace our authentic selves, we can inspire and uplift those around us.

The third verse introduces different characters from various cultures: Japanese, Russian, and Chinese. It showcases how people from different backgrounds break free from societal norms and express themselves in their own unique ways.

While the lyrics of Walk Like an Egyptian may seem lighthearted and catchy, they carry a deeper message of self-empowerment and breaking free from societal constraints. The song encourages listeners to find the courage to be themselves and celebrate their individuality.

This song holds a special place in my heart as it reminds me of my teenage years. I vividly remember dancing around my bedroom, mimicking the iconic Egyptian walk, and feeling a sense of freedom and liberation. It was a time when I was discovering myself and learning to embrace my individuality.

Walk Like an Egyptian has also been featured in various movies and TV shows over the years, further solidifying its place in popular culture. Its infectious melody and relatable message continue to resonate with listeners of all ages.

In conclusion, Walk Like an Egyptian by The Bangles is not just a catchy pop song. It carries a powerful message of self-expression and breaking free from societal norms. Its playful lyrics and infectious melody have captivated audiences for decades, making it an enduring classic. So next time you hear this song, don’t be afraid to embrace your inner Egyptian and walk confidently in your own unique way.
