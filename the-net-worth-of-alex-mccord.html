Alex McCord: A Brief Biography

Alex McCord is a well-known name in the entertainment industry, particularly for her role in the reality TV series ‘The Real Housewives of New York City’ on Bravo.

Estimates suggest that Alex’s net worth ranges from $400,000 to $2.4 million, attributed to her various sources of income. Apart from her acting career, she has also worked as a voice actor, graphic designer, and author.

Alex McCord’s Net Worth: $400,000

Born on October 1, 1973 in Washington, DC, United States, Alex grew up in Kansas, Texas. She briefly attended Northwestern University before dropping out.

Alex’s net worth received a significant boost from her appearances in various feature films such as ‘Uptown Girls’ (2003), ‘The Architect’ (2006), and ‘The Warrior Class’ (2007). She also made notable television appearances in shows like ‘Cyberchase’ and ‘One Life to Live’, further augmenting her wealth. Her most prominent television role has been in ‘The Real Housewives of New York City’, which has been on air since 2008.

Beyond acting, Alex has had a successful career as a graphic designer, working with reputable companies like Limited Brands and Victoria’s Secret, which has contributed to her net worth. She has also co-authored a book on parenting titled ‘Little Kids, Big City’ with her husband.

Alex has made guest appearances on various shows including ‘Good Morning America’, ‘Access Hollywood’, and ‘New York Live’. Due to her popularity, it is expected that her wealth and fame will continue to rise.

Currently married to Simon van Kempen, a hotel general manager, Alex has two sons named Francois (born in 2003) and Johan (born in 2010). The family resides in Cobble Hill, Brooklyn, United States.
