The Untold Truth Of Ashley 'Minnie' Ross

Ashley "Minnie" Ross was a Tennessee native who rose to fame in 2016, when she joined the Little Women franchise. It didn't take long for fans to fall in love with her charming and positive personality. 

Though Ross never got married or had kids, she revealed in 2019, that she was dating Atlanta-based music producer Slickbeatz, according to People. "I'm crushing on this guy every day," she shared on Instagram. "...loving me some him."

Her romance with Slickbeatz was a sunny shift from the relationship drama that previously played out on the show. According to MadameNoire, Ross promoted a "fake relationship and not one but two fake pregnancies with rapper Pastor Troy" on the show. She eventually told the truth and apologized on the show. "It feels like a weight has been lifted off of me," she said after her confession. "This is the first step toward building a new Minnie."
