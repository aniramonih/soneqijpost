The Weeknd Disses Bella Hadid In New Song Six Feet Under? Listen Hollywood Life

It’s only been a couple of weeks since The Weeknd, 26, and Bella Hadid, 20, called it quits, but the “Starboy” singer might have already released a nasty breakup song. An official music video hasn’t been released for “Six Feet Under” yet, so we’re relying on the scathing lyrics to tell the story. The Weeknd starts by describing a woman who’s as cold as ice. “She don’t get emotional, kill off all her feelings, that’s why she ain’t approachable.” Reports claimed the model and the musician broke up due to conflicting schedules, but this song suggest there were deeper issues.

“Six Feet Under” also features rapper Future, who may be jumping on the vengeful train to diss his ex-girlfriend as well, Ciara. The hip-hop couple didn’t have the friendliest breakup either, considering Future allegedly cheated on his fiancée, forcing her to call off the engagement in 2014. Future and Ciara share one child together, a son named Future Zahir Wilburn, so let’s hope he doesn’t hear “Six Feet Under” any time soon.
