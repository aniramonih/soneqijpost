What is Tracy Lawrence's Net Worth?

Tracy Lawrence is an American country music artist who has a net worth of $9 million. Tracy Lawrence was born in Atlanta, Texas in January 1968. He started his career working at a country music restaurant before being signed by Atlantic Records. His debut studio album Sticks and Stones was released in 1991 and reached #10 on the US Country chart. Lawrence's album Alibis was released in 1993 and reached #5 on the US and Canadian Country charts and #25 on the Billboard 200 chart. His album I See It Now was released in 1994 and reached #3 on the US Country chart.

Lawrence's albums Time Marches On in 1996 and The Coast Is Clear in 1997 both reached #4 on that chart. He released the albums Lessons Learned in 2000 and Tracy Lawrence in 2001. His album Strong was released in 2004 and reached #2 on the US Country chart. Lawrence has also released the albums For the Love in 2007, The Rock in 2009, The Singer in 2011, and Headlights, Taillights and Radios in 2013. He has had several #1 hits including "Sticks and Stones", "Alibis", "Can't Break It to My Heart", "My Second Home", "If the Good Die Young", "Texas Tornado", "Time Marches On", and "Find Out Who Your Friends Are".
