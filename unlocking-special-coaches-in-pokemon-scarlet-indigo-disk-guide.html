Unlocking Special Coaches in Pokemon Scarlet: Indigo Disk Guide - SarkariResult

News: To access Special Coaches in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet’s League Club, you must first complete the main storyline of the game, known as The Indigo Disk. After finishing the main narrative, the Special Coaches feature will unlock and become accessible within the League Club.

Spend Blueberry Points (BP)

Inviting Special Coaches to the League Club requires Blueberry Points (BP). Access the League Club’s PC and spend 200 BP to invite a Coach. Each Coach, representing distinct characters from the Paldea region, offers unique interactions and can be summoned using this method.

Interact and Befriend

After inviting a Coach, it’s important to regularly interact with them during your visits to the League Club. Engage in conversations and battles with the Coaches to enhance your friendship level. Cultivating a strong relationship with the Coaches is vital as it paves the way for unlocking additional features within the game.

Summon Coaches

For increased interaction opportunities with the Coaches, consider spending an additional 200 BP to summon them directly to the League Club. Doing so provides more chances to engage in conversations and battles, thereby reinforcing your bond and fostering a stronger relationship with them.

Pokémon Trades

Following three separate conversations with a Coach, they might propose to trade one of their Pokémon with you. These traded Pokémon are distinguished by a special Partner Ribbon, signifying their distinctiveness. To finalize the trade, you’ll be required to spend 600 BP per Coach.

Gold Border

Continuing your interactions and fostering friendships with the Coaches will lead to them having a gold border on the Special Coaches menu. This golden border signifies their readiness to engage in Pokémon trades. As you progress and strengthen these friendships, more trade opportunities will unlock, encouraging further interaction and bond-building with the Coaches.

Unlock Conditions

Please take note that specific Coaches might have distinct unlock conditions. For instance, to access Miriam, completing her befriending quest at the Academy is necessary. Similarly, gaining access to Academy Professors as Special Coaches mandates the completion of all subject courses and exams. Paying attention to these conditions and meeting them is crucial for unlocking additional Coaches.

Engaging, fostering friendships, and trading with Special Coaches in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet’s The Indigo Disk is an enjoyable and rewarding aspect of the game. By following the aforementioned steps, you can unlock more characters within the League Club and relish their unique features. Continuously explore the Paldea region and expand your collection of Coaches to enhance your gameplay experience.
