Kris Nova Was a Transgender Woman!

If you knew Kris Nova before her death, you probably might have wondered, at least once, about her sexuality as she had a little masculinity in her appearance. As many of us thought, we confirm that she was a transgender woman.

Kris, herself, wrote that she is trans in the author’s description of her book,Hacking Capitalism: Modeling, Humans, Computers, and Money. The description reads,

Kris Nóva is a computer scientist, alpinist, public speaker and transgender advocate best known for her work on Linux and Kubernetes.

Previously, she also opened up about her sexuality in an interview with Tagg Magazine. When asked what brought her to open source and computer science, she told,

The society we live in convinces transgender people of one thing—they aren’t worth existing. Most of us have families that abandoned us which meant we learnt the rules of the world on our own. For me it was no different. As I struggled with understanding my own existence as a transwoman, my relationship with the workplace and the world I lived in I started exploring avenues that would help me identify.
