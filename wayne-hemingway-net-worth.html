What is Wayne Hemingway's Net Worth?

Wayne Hemingway is an English fashion designer who has a net worth of $20 million. Wayne Hemingway was born in Morecambe, England in January 1961. He is the co-founder of Red or Dead as well as the chairman of the South Coast Design Forum and a chair of Building For Life. Wayne and his wife Gerardine Hemingway founded Red or Dead in London in 1982.

The company designs shoes, bags, watches, and spectacles. The Hemingways sold Red or Dead in 1999 and set up Hemingway Design. Wayne Hemingway has also authored the books Kitsch, Just Above The Mantelpiece: Mass-Market Masterpieces, Mass Market Classics: A Celebration of Everyday Design, Cocktail Shakes Lava Lamps Tuppe, The Home Buyers Guide Counter-pack, Richard Okon: Prefab Publisher, Place to Live, and The Vintage Fashion Bible: The Complete Guide to Buying and Styling Vintage Fashion from the 1920s to 1990s.
