Who Is Anna Kendrick Dating? All About the 'Love Life' Star

Anna Kendrick and Bill Hader have officially called it quits after dating for two years. Stick around for all the known details.

There's nothing, and we mean nothing, that Anna Kendrick couldn't make cool — including sweatpants, watching old reruns of Chopped, and secret relationships.

Hold on, what's the last one? Turns out, the Pitch Perfect star was quietly in a relationship for the past two years with comedian Bill Hader. But sadly, in June 2022, news broke that Anna and Bill are officially dunzo.

So, what do we know about Anna's dating life, including her previous relationships? Read on for more.

Who is Anna Kendrick dating?

As mentioned, Anna was most recently linked to Bill Hader. The couple — who met in 2017 on the set of the Disney Plus original film Noelle together — were notoriously private about their romance.

But it appears that neither party will have to keep their relationship hidden from the public eye any longer: In late June 2022, Entertainment Tonight reported that Anna and Bill officially broke up.

No further details regarding their split have been revealed at this time, but the news comes just two months after the Saturday Night Live alum shut down rumors that Anna would appear on his acclaimed HBO dark comedy series, Barry.

The pair reportedly started dating in 2020 and managed to keep their relationship a secret for over a year. In January 2022, a source spoke with People and revealed the COVID-19 pandemic allowed them to keep their romance quiet.

One month later, another source told People that Anna and Bill were a "great couple."

"You can see the chemistry when they're together. They have a really fun rapport, very sarcastic and funny but all coming from a place of love," the source said. "You'd think she'd be the one kind of running the show, but she's pretty quiet around him."

"They're affectionate but it's the way they look at one another when the other is talking, you can see they really love each other."

Anna Kendrick previously dated Edgar Wright and Ben Richardson.

Prior to Bill, Anna dated filmmaker Edgar Wright. The pair met in 2009 on set of Scott Pilgrim vs. the World — Edgar wrote and directed the film, while Anna starred as Stacey Pilgrim. The couple dated for four years until calling it quits in 2013.

By 2014, Anna began dating cinematographer Ben Richardson; he is best known for his work on The Fault in Our Stars, Wind River, and Mare of Easttown. Not much is known about their relationship, but Anna and Ben split in 2020 after six years together.
