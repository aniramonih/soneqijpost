Who is Scott McCurleys wife Colleen?

Scott McCurley is the current linebackers coach for the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League.

Scott McCurley was born in New Castle, Pennsylvania.

Scott McCurley graduated from Mohawk High school in Bessemer, Pennsylvania and attended the University of Pittsburgh.

During college Scott McCurley played linebacker with the Pittsburgh Panthers football team. Following graduation he remained with the program as a defensive graduate assistant.

Scott McCurley originally joined the Packers in 2006 as an intern before being named Coaching Administrator the following year.

On February 10, 2014, it was announced that Scott McCurley was promoted to linebackers coach of the Packers.

After the departure of Kevin Greene as outside linebackers coach, it was announced that Winston Moss would take over as coach of both the inside and outside linebackers, and that Scott McCurley will assist in whichever area he is needed.

Who is Scott McCurley’s wife Colleen?

Scott McCurley is married to Colleen (Crisi) McCurley and they have two children.

Scott McCurley and Colleen reside in De Pere, Wisconsin.
