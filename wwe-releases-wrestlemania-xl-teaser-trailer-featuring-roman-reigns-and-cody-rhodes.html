WWE Releases WrestleMania XL Teaser Trailer Featuring Roman Reigns and Cody Rhodes

WrestleMania 40 is set to take place on April 6 and April 7 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. This year’s show is going to be headlined by Roman Reigns and Cody Rhodes.

Apart from this, there are several great matches shaping up for the biggest show of the year. Seth Rollins will be defending his World Heavyweight Championship and Bayley will also challenge IYO SKY for the WWE Women’s Championship.

Given that WrestleMania 40 is over a month away, WWE just released the teaser trailer for the event. The trailer showcases the four biggest stars of the show The Rock, Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins, and Cody Rhodes.

All four men stared at each other as clips from the WrestleMania XL Kickoff Press Event played showing The Rock slapping Cody Rhodes and the brawl that broke out. The teaser trailer ends with the dates for the two nights of WrestleMania 40.

The teaser trailer indicated that there could be some sort of tag match that could take place at WrestleMania 40 featuring the above-mentioned four Superstars. However, we will have to wait and see how the Road to WrestleMania plays out for all four men.
